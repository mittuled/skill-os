## Q2 Budget Implications

**Organic Search** is the highest-attributed channel at 35.7% of normalized revenue — driven by 3 of 5 deals. This supports increasing SEO and content investment.

**Paid Social** drove only 1 deal (OPP-204, largest at $60K) but contributed just 2.0% after attribution distribution. The single large deal inflates raw ROAS — run a 30-day spend experiment to verify before increasing budget.

**Referral** converts at $9.6K attributed from a single $24K deal — highest attribution efficiency. A formal referral programme could unlock significant pipeline at low cost.

## Attribution Model Comparison Note

W-Shaped is appropriate for Meridian AI's B2B sales cycle. If the marketing team has debates about which model to use, run all 5 deals through Linear and U-Shaped as a secondary view — organic search and content will show even higher share, reinforcing the investment case.
