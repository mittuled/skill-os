# Scoring Rubric: positioning-legal-reviewer

Evaluates the completeness of a positioning legal review covering claims inventory, substantiation, and FTC/Lanham Act compliance.

## Criteria

| # | Criterion | Weight | Description |
|---|-----------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Claims Inventory | 30% | Completeness of cataloging all factual claims, comparative statements, and implied representations with accurate categorization |
| 2 | Substantiation Review | 40% | Rigour of evidence verification for each factual claim including testing methodology and sample adequacy |
| 3 | Compliance Recommendation | 30% | Quality of flagged items, proposed alternative language, and FTC/Lanham Act violation identification |

| **Total** | | **100%** | |

## Scale

Each criterion is scored **0-10**:
- **0**: No evidence / completely absent
- **5**: Partially present with significant gaps
- **10**: Fully present, comprehensive, no gaps

**Composite Score** = Σ (criterion score × weight)

## Grade Bands

| Grade | Composite Score | Label | Description | Recommended Action |
|-------|----------------|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| A+ | 9.0 – 10.0 | Exceptional | All claims inventoried and categorized, substantiation verified for each, compliant alternatives provided for all flagged items | Approve positioning materials |
| A | 8.0 – 8.9 | Strong | Claims thoroughly inventoried, substantiation verified for major claims, alternatives provided | Approve with minor language adjustments |
| B | 7.0 – 7.9 | Good | Most claims inventoried, substantiation checked for primary claims, most flagged items have alternatives | Approve with revisions before publication |
| C | 5.0 – 6.9 | Adequate | Claims partially inventoried, substantiation gaps for some claims, alternatives incomplete | Hold: complete substantiation review before publication |
| D | 3.0 – 4.9 | Weak | Significant claims missed in inventory, substantiation not verified, few alternatives provided | Block publication: major review required |
| F | 0.0 – 2.9 | Failing | No systematic claims review performed | Block publication: full review from scratch |

## Signal Tables

### Claims Inventory

| Score | Evidence |
|-------|----------|
| 9-10 | Every factual claim, performance assertion, superlative, comparative statement, and implied representation cataloged. Each classified as factual, opinion, puffery, or comparative with rationale. Full-context review (landing page, visuals, footnotes) for net impression. |
| 7-8 | Major claims cataloged and classified, most implied representations identified, context reviewed |
| 5-6 | Explicit claims cataloged but implied representations or contextual claims missed |
| 3-4 | Partial inventory covering obvious claims only |
| 0-2 | No claims inventory performed |

### Substantiation Review

| Score | Evidence |
|-------|----------|
| 9-10 | Each factual claim verified against competent and reliable evidence. Performance claims backed by testing with documented methodology and adequate sample sizes. Comparative claims verified as accurate, current, and non-misleading. Testimonials verified for typicality or disclaimers added. |
| 7-8 | Major claims substantiated, testing methodology reviewed, most comparisons verified |
| 5-6 | Some claims substantiated but evidence quality not assessed, comparisons partially verified |
| 3-4 | Substantiation requested but not verified against FTC evidence standards |
| 0-2 | No substantiation review performed |

### Compliance Recommendation

| Score | Evidence |
|-------|----------|
| 9-10 | Every non-substantiated claim flagged with specific FTC/Lanham Act violation cited. Compliant alternative language provided for each. Missing disclosures identified (material connections, typical results). Sector-specific advertising rules addressed. |
| 7-8 | Flagged items have violation citations and alternative language, most disclosure gaps identified |
| 5-6 | Items flagged but alternative language incomplete or violation citations generic |
| 3-4 | Some items flagged without alternative language or specific legal basis |
| 0-2 | No compliance recommendations provided |
