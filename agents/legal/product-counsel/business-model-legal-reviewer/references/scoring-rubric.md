# Scoring Rubric: business-model-legal-reviewer

Evaluates the completeness and rigour of a business model legal review covering regulatory mapping, licensing analysis, liability assessment, and compliance integration.

## Criteria

| # | Criterion | Weight | Description |
|---|-----------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Regulatory Surface Mapping | 25% | Completeness of identifying regulated activities, data flows, and regulatory touchpoints across all jurisdictions |
| 2 | Licensing Analysis | 25% | Depth of licensing and registration requirements identification with jurisdictional coverage |
| 3 | Liability Assessment | 30% | Quality of liability exposure analysis including platform liability, consumer protection, and indemnification |
| 4 | Compliance Integration | 20% | Actionability of translated regulatory requirements into business constraints and ongoing obligations |

| **Total** | | **100%** | |

## Scale

Each criterion is scored **0-10**:
- **0**: No evidence / completely absent
- **5**: Partially present with significant gaps
- **10**: Fully present, comprehensive, no gaps

**Composite Score** = Σ (criterion score × weight)

## Grade Bands

| Grade | Composite Score | Label | Description | Recommended Action |
|-------|----------------|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| A+ | 9.0 – 10.0 | Exceptional | All regulated activities identified across jurisdictions, licensing matrix complete, liability analysis covers platform/consumer/contractual dimensions with mitigations | Approve business model with standard monitoring |
| A | 8.0 – 8.9 | Strong | Comprehensive mapping with minor jurisdiction gaps, licensing requirements identified, liability analysis thorough | Approve with minor compliance items to track |
| B | 7.0 – 7.9 | Good | Major regulatory areas covered, most licensing requirements identified, liability assessment present but missing some dimensions | Approve with conditions: address gaps within 60 days |
| C | 5.0 – 6.9 | Adequate | Core US regulations identified, licensing partially mapped, liability assessment limited to obvious exposure | Conditional: complete international analysis before market entry |
| D | 3.0 – 4.9 | Weak | Significant regulatory gaps, licensing requirements unclear, liability assessment superficial | Revise: major gaps must be addressed before business model adoption |
| F | 0.0 – 2.9 | Failing | No regulatory mapping, no licensing analysis, no liability assessment | Restart review from scratch |

## Signal Tables

### Regulatory Surface Mapping

| Score | Evidence |
|-------|----------|
| 9-10 | All regulated activities identified (financial services, insurance, healthcare, education), data flows mapped to regulatory triggers, Section 230 applicability analysed, EU DSA/DMA obligations assessed, money transmitter analysis complete |
| 7-8 | Major regulated activities identified, data flow regulatory triggers documented, most jurisdictions covered |
| 5-6 | Primary regulated activities noted, US-focused analysis, international obligations mentioned but not analysed |
| 3-4 | Generic regulatory awareness without specific activity-to-regulation mapping |
| 0-2 | No regulatory surface mapping performed |

### Licensing Analysis

| Score | Evidence |
|-------|----------|
| 9-10 | Licensing requirements matrix with jurisdiction, licence type, cost, timeline, and application process for each required licence; state-by-state analysis for US; international equivalents identified |
| 7-8 | Major licensing requirements identified with jurisdictions, costs estimated, timelines projected |
| 5-6 | Licensing categories identified but jurisdiction-specific analysis incomplete |
| 3-4 | General awareness of licensing needs without specific requirements or jurisdictions |
| 0-2 | No licensing analysis performed |

### Liability Assessment

| Score | Evidence |
|-------|----------|
| 9-10 | Product liability, professional liability, platform liability (Section 230 with feature-level analysis), consumer protection obligations (warranty, refund, disclosure), indemnification structures all assessed with risk ratings and mitigation recommendations |
| 7-8 | Major liability dimensions covered, risk ratings assigned, most mitigations identified |
| 5-6 | Liability categories identified but analysis lacks depth or misses platform liability nuances |
| 3-4 | Generic liability concerns noted without specific analysis or risk ratings |
| 0-2 | No liability assessment performed |

### Compliance Integration

| Score | Evidence |
|-------|----------|
| 9-10 | Regulatory requirements translated into specific ToS requirements, disclosure obligations, compliance monitoring processes, and reporting obligations with owners and deadlines |
| 7-8 | Most requirements translated into actionable items, monitoring needs identified |
| 5-6 | Requirements listed but not translated into specific business constraints or implementation guidance |
| 3-4 | General compliance recommendations without integration into business operations |
| 0-2 | No compliance integration performed |
