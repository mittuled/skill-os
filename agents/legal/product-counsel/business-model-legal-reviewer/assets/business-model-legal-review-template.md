## Business Model Legal Map

[Document the revenue model, value chain, parties involved, data flows, and regulatory touchpoints.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: "Marketplace model connecting freelance professionals with SMBs. Revenue from 15% transaction fee. Data flows: user PII collected at registration, payment data processed via Stripe, professional credentials stored for verification. Regulatory touchpoints: money transmission (transaction facilitation), professional licensing (credential verification), FTC disclosure (marketplace fees)."
- Bad: "We are a marketplace."
- Format: Table with Model Element, Description, Regulatory Touchpoint, Jurisdiction]

## Licensing Requirements Matrix

[Licensing, registration, or permit requirements by jurisdiction.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: Table with Jurisdiction, Licence Type, Requirement Source, Cost, Timeline, Application Process, Current Status
- Bad: "We may need some licences"
- Format: Matrix covering all applicable jurisdictions with specific licence types]

## Liability Assessment

[Liability exposure analysis across all relevant dimensions.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: "Platform liability: Section 230 protection applies to user-generated reviews but not to algorithmically curated 'featured professionals' (editorial function). Risk rating: Medium. Mitigation: separate curated content from user-generated content in product architecture."
- Bad: "Section 230 protects us"
- Format: Table with Liability Type, Applicable Law, Exposure Level (High/Medium/Low), Current Mitigation, Residual Risk, Recommended Action]

## Compliance Requirements

[Actionable business constraints derived from regulatory analysis.

- Format: Table with Requirement, Source Regulation, Implementation Need, Owner, Deadline]
