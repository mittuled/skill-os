## Pre-Launch (T-7 to T-2 days)

### Messaging and Content
- [ ] Press release or blog post final draft approved
- [ ] Social media copy written and scheduled (Twitter/X, LinkedIn)
- [ ] Email announcement copy approved (customer and prospect segments)
- [ ] In-app announcement written and staged
- [ ] Help documentation updated and reviewed
- [ ] Sales enablement materials delivered (battle card, talk track, one-pager)

### Partner and Channel Readiness
- [ ] Partner briefing completed: [Partners notified, date]
- [ ] Reseller / agency comms sent: [Date]
- [ ] Integration partner notifications sent (if applicable)

### Internal Readiness
- [ ] Support team briefed; top 5 FAQ written and in knowledge base
- [ ] CS team briefed; high-impact account list prepared
- [ ] Sales team briefed; objection handlers updated
