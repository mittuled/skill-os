# Framework: GTM Activation Execution

Reference framework for executing the launch activation plan with cross-functional coordination.

## Activation Phase Model

| Phase | Duration | Focus | Key Metric |
|-------|----------|-------|------------|
| Pre-activation | T-3 to T-1 days | Readiness confirmation, final staging, team alignment | All checklist items green |
| Day-zero activation | T+0 | Coordinated channel launch, real-time monitoring | Channels firing on schedule |
| Sustain wave | T+1 to T+5 days | Day 2+ content drops, channel optimisation, early signal response | Engagement metrics trending |
| Handoff | T+5 to T+7 days | Summary, performance report, sustained-motion handoff | Handoff document accepted |

## Real-Time Monitoring Dashboard

Track these metrics during the first 48 hours:

| Channel | Metric | Healthy Signal | Action if Below |
|---------|--------|---------------|-----------------|
| Email | Open rate | >25% for launch segment | Revise subject line; resend to non-openers at T+24h |
| Email | Click rate | >4% | Review CTA clarity and email layout |
| Blog | Unique visitors (first 24h) | >2x average post | Boost social distribution; add internal promotion |
| In-app | Announcement click-through | >15% | Revise copy or placement; check targeting rules |
| Social | Impressions | >3x average post | Maintain cadence; no action needed if above |
| Social | Engagement rate | >2% | Revise hook; test alternative copy variant |
| Sales | Inbound demo requests (first 48h) | Above baseline | If significantly above, alert sales capacity planning |
| Product | Feature activation rate | Defined per launch | If below target, review onboarding flow or in-app guidance |

## Daily Activation Standup Format

Run daily during launch week (15 minutes max):

1. **Channel status** (2 min per channel): What fired, what is blocked, early metrics
2. **Blockers** (3 min): Surface any issues requiring immediate resolution
3. **Adjustments** (3 min): Channel emphasis shifts based on performance data
4. **Tomorrow's actions** (2 min): Confirm next-day content drops and milestones

## Activation Summary Report Structure

Due within 48 hours of launch week close:

1. **Launch overview**: What launched, tier, target audience, dates
2. **Execution scorecard**: Checklist items completed on time / delayed / blocked
3. **Channel performance**: Metrics per channel vs. targets
4. **What worked**: Top-performing channels, messaging, or tactics with evidence
5. **What did not work**: Underperforming areas with root cause analysis
6. **Recommendations**: Adjustments for sustained marketing phase
7. **Handoff items**: Ongoing campaigns, nurture sequences, and owners
