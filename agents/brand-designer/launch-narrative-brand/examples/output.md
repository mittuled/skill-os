## Narrative Arc

| Element | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Hook** | Your engineers spend 3 hours a week waiting for deployments. That's 156 hours a year of lost productivity per engineer. |
| **Tension** | The tools that are supposed to speed you up are slowing you down. |
| **Resolution** | Flowline cuts your deployment cycle in half — automated CI/CD for engineering teams who move fast. |
| **CTA** | Start free — deploy your first pipeline in 10 minutes |

**Proof points**:
1. Acme Corp reduced deployment time from 45 minutes to 8 minutes
2. 87% of customers go live in under 2 weeks
3. Zero-config GitHub Actions integration

## Consistency Rules

1. Use the same hero image across website, Product Hunt, and OG social cards — identical visual anchors trust
2. Core message ("cuts your deployment cycle in half") must appear verbatim on all high-attention surfaces
3. Testimonials must be from real customers with explicit permission to use their name and company
4. Demo GIF must match the live product — no aspirational features or screens not yet in production
