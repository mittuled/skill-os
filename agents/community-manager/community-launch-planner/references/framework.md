# Framework: community-launch-planner

Defines the phased launch model, readiness criteria, and platform selection matrix for community platform launches.

## Launch Phase Model

| Phase | Duration | Goal | Key Activities | Success Gate |
|-------|----------|------|---------------|-------------|
| Pre-launch seeding | 4–8 weeks before launch | Build seed content and founding cohort | Recruit 20–50 founding members, seed 20+ posts, configure platform, train moderators | Platform ready, seed content live, founding members active |
| Soft launch | 1–2 weeks before public | Validate experience with controlled traffic | Invite waitlist (100–500), stress-test moderation, fix onboarding gaps | Onboarding completion > 60%, no critical bugs, moderators staffed |
| Public launch | Launch day | Maximum awareness and activation | Announce on all channels, monitor real-time, execute contingency plans | First-day signups hit target, no major incidents |
| Post-launch growth | 4 weeks after | Sustain momentum and hand off | Daily monitoring, re-engage early churners, transition to ongoing ops | 30-day retention > 35%, team ops running without escalation |

## Platform Selection Matrix

| Platform | Best For | Key Capabilities | Limitations | Setup Complexity |
|----------|----------|-----------------|-------------|-----------------|
| Slack / Discord | Developer communities, async chat | Real-time messaging, channels, bots | Thread discoverability, search limits | Low |
| Circle | Community-first SaaS | Content + discussion + events in one | Cost at scale, no real-time feel | Medium |
| Discourse | Technical communities, long-form | Powerful search, threading, gamification | Dated UX, requires self-hosting or managed | Medium–High |
| Reddit (subreddit) | Public/open communities | Built-in discovery, voting | Limited customization, brand control | Low |
| Mighty Networks | Courses + community hybrid | Memberships, gating, mobile app | Premium pricing, learning curve | Medium |

## Launch Readiness Checklist

### Platform Configuration
- [ ] Platform provisioned and branded (logo, colors, welcome message)
- [ ] Member roles and permissions configured
- [ ] Onboarding flow tested end-to-end (signup → first post < 3 steps)
- [ ] Welcome bot or DM configured for new members
- [ ] Notification settings configured to avoid alert fatigue

### Content Seeding (minimum before launch)
- [ ] Community purpose statement pinned
- [ ] Community guidelines published
- [ ] 5+ introductory discussion threads started by founding members
- [ ] 3+ resource posts with genuinely useful content
- [ ] Welcome thread where new members can introduce themselves
- [ ] At least 1 recurring ritual scheduled (weekly AMA, feedback thread)

### Moderation Coverage
- [ ] Moderators scheduled for 24/7 coverage for first 72 hours
- [ ] Moderation guidelines document shared with mod team
- [ ] Escalation path defined for serious violations
- [ ] Keyword filters configured for spam/abusive content
- [ ] Member reporting mechanism tested

### Cross-functional Alignment
- [ ] Launch announcement drafted and approved
- [ ] Support team briefed on expected community-related tickets
- [ ] Product team notified for feature questions
- [ ] Social media posts scheduled around launch
- [ ] Email announcement ready for user base

## Content Seed Formula

Use the **3-2-1 seed pattern** for pre-launch content:
- **3 resource posts**: Genuinely useful guides, tips, or reference material
- **2 discussion starters**: Open-ended questions that invite member opinions
- **1 ritual thread**: The first instance of the recurring weekly/monthly ritual (e.g., "Introduce yourself")

Minimum 10 posts total. Founding members should author at least 40% to model peer contribution.

## Success Metrics by Launch Phase

| Phase | Metric | Target |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Soft launch | Onboarding completion rate | > 60% |
| Soft launch | Bug/friction reports addressed | 100% before public |
| Launch day | New member signups | [Set by community team] |
| Launch day | Activation rate (first post within 24h) | > 30% |
| Week 1 | Daily active members | > 20% of launched members |
| Month 1 | 30-day cohort retention | > 35% |
