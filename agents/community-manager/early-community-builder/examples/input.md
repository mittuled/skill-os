# Early Community Building Plan: Flowline Developers

## Scenario

Flowline is preparing for its public launch on April 15, 2026. The community manager needs to recruit 30 founding members for the Discord community before the launch. The target persona is B2B SaaS engineers who care about CI/CD quality. Founding members will be sourced from the beta user email list, Twitter followers, and personal networks. The community manager wants a full 12-week plan covering outreach, norms setup, content seeding, and rituals.
