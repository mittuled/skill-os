# Framework: community-led-growth-designer

Defines the growth readiness model, lever selection matrix, programme design patterns, and success metrics for community-led growth programmes.

## Growth Readiness Thresholds

All thresholds must be met before activating growth programmes. Launching growth on an unhealthy community multiplies churn.

| Health Metric | Minimum Threshold | Ideal Before Scaling |
|--------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| Monthly Active Member rate | > 20% of total members | > 35% |
| 30-day cohort retention | > 35% | > 50% |
| Net Sentiment Score | > 50 | > 65 |
| Avg response time | < 24 hours | < 4 hours |
| Moderation capacity | Handles current volume | Has 30% headroom |

## Growth Lever Matrix

| Lever | Description | Best For | Effort | Expected CAC Reduction |
|-------|-------------|----------|--------|----------------------|
| Ambassador programme | Selected members represent brand | Established communities with clear advocates | High | 40–60% vs. paid |
| Referral incentives | Members earn rewards for referrals | Communities with transactional member benefit | Medium | 30–50% vs. paid |
| User-generated content | Members create shareable content | Communities with strong expertise or creativity | Medium | Variable |
| Expert-led events | Members run AMAs, workshops | Technical or professional communities | Medium | Indirect (brand equity) |
| Community-to-product loops | Member feedback → product improvements → members share improvements | Product-led communities | High | High (viral coefficient) |
| Affiliate/partner network | External creators or brands promote | Consumer communities | Low–Medium | 20–40% vs. paid |

## Programme Design Patterns

### Ambassador Programme Structure

| Component | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Eligibility criteria | Who can join | Active member for 90+ days, 10+ posts, positive sentiment |
| Benefits | What ambassadors receive | Early access, swag, event invitations, direct line to team |
| Obligations | What ambassadors commit to | 2 posts/month, attend 1 event/quarter, respond to referral requests |
| Recognition | How ambassadors are celebrated | Profile badge, dedicated channel, quarterly spotlight |
| Success metrics | How programme is measured | Referrals per ambassador, content created, event attendance driven |

### Referral Programme Structure

| Component | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Trigger event | What activates a referral opportunity | First successful product outcome |
| Incentive type | Extrinsic vs. intrinsic | Access upgrade, recognition, co-branding |
| Referral mechanism | How referrals are tracked | Unique invite link per member |
| Conversion tracking | Measuring referral quality | Referred member 30-day retention vs. organic baseline |
| Programme cap | Prevents abuse | Max 10 referrals/member/month for premium incentive tier |

## Community-to-Product Growth Loop

```
Member uses product
      ↓
Member has outcome worth sharing
      ↓
Community celebrates outcome (post, case study, feature)
      ↓
Celebration reaches prospective member's feed
      ↓
Prospective member joins community / signs up for product
      ↓
New member uses product → loop repeats
```

**Viral coefficient (k) target:** k > 0.5 for meaningful organic contribution; k > 1.0 for self-sustaining growth

**Formula:** k = (invites sent per active member) × (conversion rate of invite to signup)

## Success Metrics by Programme Type

| Programme | Primary KPI | Secondary KPIs | Review Cadence |
|-----------|------------|---------------|---------------|
| Ambassador | Referrals per ambassador per quarter | Content created, event attendance driven | Monthly |
| Referral | Referred member 30-day retention | Referral conversion rate, CAC vs. organic | Bi-weekly |
| UGC | Monthly pieces created | Views, shares, pipeline influenced | Monthly |
| Expert events | Attendance per event | Post-event community sign-ups, NPS | Per event |
| Product loop | Viral coefficient (k) | Organic MoM growth rate, attribution | Monthly |
