# Community Health Assessment: Flowline Developer Community

## Scenario

Flowline's developer community on Discord has been live for 8 months. The community manager is conducting the quarterly health assessment ahead of a potential ambassador programme launch. Leadership wants to know if the community is healthy enough to support a growth push, or if intervention is needed first.
