## Executive Summary

Q3 plan targets $450,000 in new revenue from 10 closed deals. Working backward through the funnel, the plan requires 321 MQLs from a $180K budget across four channels. Current channel allocation is projected to deliver approximately 87% of required MQL volume — a $22K budget supplement or webinar frequency increase is recommended to close the gap.

## MQL Definition

**Lead Score Threshold:** 45 points
**Handoff SLA:** 4 hours from MQL trigger

**Behavioral Criteria (scoring triggers):**
- Visited pricing page 2+ times → 20 points
- Downloaded case study or ROI calculator → 15 points
- Attended live product demo → 25 points

**Firmographic Criteria (disqualifiers if not met):**
- Company size: 100–2,000 employees
- Industry: SaaS, FinTech, or HealthTech
- Title: VP, Director, or C-level

**Disqualification rules:** Auto-disqualify competitors, students, consultants. Route to nurture if score 20–44.

## Risk Flags and Recommendations

1. **MQL coverage at 87%** — Current allocation projects 280 MQLs vs. 321 required. Recommended remediation: add 2 additional webinars in July ($20K budget) or increase paid search by $15K targeting bottom-funnel keywords.
2. **Content/SEO velocity lag** — SEO investments made in Q3 will produce MQLs primarily in Q4. Q3 near-term pipeline relies on paid search and webinars; ensure these channels are fully activated by Week 1.
3. **Experiment reserve deployment** — $31.5K experiment budget should be deployed on highest-performing test signal by Week 6. Candidates: partner co-marketing, LinkedIn thought leadership ads, intent-data outbound.
