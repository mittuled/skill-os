# Framework: gtm-planner-marketing

Defines the ICP-to-channel mapping, channel priority matrix model, GTM phasing structure, and budget allocation methodology for building a comprehensive go-to-market plan.

## ICP-to-Channel Mapping

Map ICP attention patterns to acquisition channels before selecting the channel mix.

| ICP Attribute | Where They Consume Information | Implied Channel Priority |
|--------------|-------------------------------|--------------------------|
| Enterprise (500+ employees) | Industry analyst reports, peer referrals, LinkedIn, conferences | ABM, LinkedIn ads, events, analyst relations |
| Mid-market (50–500 employees) | Search, review sites (G2/Capterra), webinars, newsletters | Paid search, review site listings, webinars, content |
| SMB (< 50 employees) | Search, social, communities, podcasts | Paid search, paid social, content SEO, community |
| Technical buyers (Eng/IT) | Docs sites, GitHub, Hacker News, developer communities | Developer content, technical SEO, community, OSS |
| Business buyers (VP/C-level) | LinkedIn, peer networks, business press, events | LinkedIn thought leadership, PR, executive events |

## Channel Priority Matrix

Score each channel and rank by expected pipeline contribution efficiency.

| Channel | ICP Reach Score (1–5) | Intent Quality (1–5) | Cost Efficiency (1–5) | Scalability (1–5) | **Composite** |
|---------|----------------------|---------------------|----------------------|-------------------|--------------|
| Branded paid search | 3 | 5 | 4 | 2 | 14/20 |
| Non-branded paid search | 4 | 3 | 3 | 4 | 14/20 |
| LinkedIn ads | 5 | 3 | 2 | 4 | 14/20 |
| Content / SEO | 4 | 2 | 5 | 5 | 16/20 |
| Email (owned) | 2 | 4 | 5 | 2 | 13/20 |
| Events / Webinars | 4 | 4 | 2 | 3 | 13/20 |
| Partner / Referral | 3 | 5 | 4 | 2 | 14/20 |
| PR / Earned media | 4 | 2 | 5 | 2 | 13/20 |
| Outbound (SDR-assisted) | 3 | 4 | 2 | 3 | 12/20 |

**Usage**: Customize scores to actual ICP data. Channels scoring ≥ 14/20 are core; 11–13 are supporting; < 11 are experimental.

## GTM Phases

| Phase | Timing | Key Activities | Success Signal |
|-------|--------|----------------|----------------|
| **Pre-launch** | 6–12 weeks before launch | Positioning finalized, assets produced, PR relationships activated, sales trained | Assets approved, sales pipeline warm |
| **Launch** | Launch week ± 1 week | Coordinated channel activation, PR push, sales outreach to ABM list | First MQLs from launch channels within 72 hrs |
| **Amplification** | Weeks 2–8 post-launch | Paid scale-up, content seeding, webinar series, partner co-marketing | CPL stable or improving; pipeline building |
| **Steady state** | Post week 8 | Sustained demand gen cadence, performance optimization, A/B testing | Predictable weekly lead volume at plan CPL |

## Budget Allocation by GTM Stage

| Stage | Typical Budget Split | Rationale |
|-------|---------------------|-----------|
| Pre-launch (6–12 wks) | 15–20% of quarterly budget | Asset production, PR, sales enablement |
| Launch (launch week) | 20–25% | Concentrated paid push, event sponsorships |
| Amplification (wks 2–8) | 40–50% | Scale what's working post-launch data |
| Steady state | Remaining | Optimized baseline spend |

## KPI Framework by GTM Objective

| Objective | Primary KPI | Secondary KPI | Reporting Cadence |
|-----------|-------------|---------------|-------------------|
| Pipeline contribution | Pipeline sourced ($) | MQLs, SQLs | Monthly |
| Brand awareness | Branded search volume | Unaided recall (surveys) | Quarterly |
| Market share | Share of voice (SOV) | Analyst ranking | Semi-annual |
| Competitive displacement | Win rate vs. named competitors | Competitive deal count | Monthly |
| New segment penetration | MQLs from new segment | Segment pipeline | Monthly |

## Cross-Functional Sign-Off Checklist

Before publishing the GTM plan, confirm alignment with each stakeholder:

| Stakeholder | What They Must Confirm |
|-------------|------------------------|
| CBO | Revenue targets, budget envelope, strategic priorities |
| VP Sales | Sales capacity assumptions, ABM account list, territory timing |
| Product Marketing | Positioning, messaging, feature availability dates |
| Customer Success | Expansion opportunity sizing, customer reference availability |
| Finance | Budget approval, ROI assumptions, reporting requirements |
| RevOps | CRM readiness, lead routing, attribution configuration |
