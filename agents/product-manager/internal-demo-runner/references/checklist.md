# Checklist: internal-demo-runner

Runs internal product demos to align stakeholders before external release.

## Pre-Flight

- [ ] All required inputs collected and accessible
- [ ] Stakeholders identified and availability confirmed
- [ ] Timeline and deadlines established

## Execution Steps

- [ ] Step 1: Define demo scope, audience, and narrative angle
- [ ] Step 2: Prepare and verify demo environment with realistic data
- [ ] Step 3: Run demo with user-perspective narration and feedback capture
- [ ] Step 4: Distribute categorised feedback within 24 hours

## Post-Completion

- [ ] All deliverables produced and stored
- [ ] Stakeholders notified of completion
- [ ] Lessons learned captured for process improvement
