# Scoring Rubric: gate-12-evaluator

Evaluates whether a product has met defined gate criteria to proceed to the next development phase.

## Criteria

| # | Criterion | Weight | Description |
|---|-----------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Gate Criteria Retrieval | 20% | Completeness of exit criteria collection and relevance validation |
| 2 | Evidence Collection | 25% | Depth and quality of artefacts gathered to demonstrate criterion fulfilment |
| 3 | Criterion Assessment | 30% | Accuracy of pass/conditional-pass/fail ratings with supporting evidence |
| 4 | Decision Documentation | 25% | Rigour of go/no-go decision record with conditions, owners, and timeline |
| **Total** | | **100%** | |

## Scale

Each criterion is scored **0-10**:
- **0**: No evidence / completely absent
- **5**: Partially present with significant gaps
- **10**: Fully present, comprehensive, no gaps

**Composite Score** = Σ (criterion score × weight)

## Grade Bands

| Grade | Composite Score | Label | Description | Recommended Action |
|-------|----------------|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| A+ | 9.0 – 10.0 | Exceptional | Exceptional performance across all criteria | Approve phase transition |
| A | 8.0 – 8.9 | Strong | Strong performance across all criteria | Approve with minor conditions |
| B | 7.0 – 7.9 | Good | Good performance across all criteria | Conditional approval with tracked items |
| C | 5.0 – 6.9 | Adequate | Adequate performance across all criteria | Defer decision pending evidence gaps |
| D | 3.0 – 4.9 | Weak | Weak performance across all criteria | Remand for significant remediation |
| F | 0.0 – 2.9 | Failing | Failing performance across all criteria | Block phase transition and reassess initiative viability |
