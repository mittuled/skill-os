# Checklist: product-feedback-ingestion

Ingests, normalises, and structures product feedback for roadmap review cycles.

## Pre-Flight

- [ ] All required inputs collected and accessible
- [ ] Stakeholders identified and availability confirmed
- [ ] Timeline and deadlines established

## Execution Steps

- [ ] Step 1: Inventory all feedback sources (support, CRM, NPS, in-app, forums)
- [ ] Step 2: Define taxonomy (category, severity, persona, product area)
- [ ] Step 3: Extract and normalise into central repository
- [ ] Step 4: Deduplicate and cluster into themes
- [ ] Step 5: Quantify impact (ARR at risk, accounts affected, support cost)
- [ ] Step 6: Synthesise top themes for roadmap review
- [ ] Step 7: Distribute and archive for longitudinal analysis

## Post-Completion

- [ ] All deliverables produced and stored
- [ ] Stakeholders notified of completion
- [ ] Lessons learned captured for process improvement
