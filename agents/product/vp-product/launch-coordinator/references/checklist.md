# Checklist: launch-coordinator

Coordinates cross-functional activities for product launch from go/no-go through GA and hypercare.

## Pre-Flight

- [ ] All required inputs collected and accessible
- [ ] Stakeholders identified and availability confirmed
- [ ] Timeline and deadlines established

## Execution Steps

- [ ] Step 1: Confirm launch readiness (engineering exit criteria, QA sign-off)
- [ ] Step 2: Assemble launch team with RACI matrix
- [ ] Step 3: Build reverse-planned launch timeline
- [ ] Step 4: Coordinate marketing and comms (blog, email, social)
- [ ] Step 5: Prepare sales enablement package
- [ ] Step 6: Stage support readiness (KB articles, runbooks, escalation)
- [ ] Step 7: Execute go/no-go ceremony **[GATE]**
- [ ] Step 8: Monitor rollout (feature flags, error rates, latency)
- [ ] Step 9: Run hypercare (48-72hr dedicated response team)

## Post-Completion

- [ ] All deliverables produced and stored
- [ ] Stakeholders notified of completion
- [ ] Lessons learned captured for process improvement
