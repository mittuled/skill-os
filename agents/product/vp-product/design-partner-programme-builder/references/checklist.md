# Checklist: design-partner-programme-builder

Builds and operates the design partner programme for early customer input during development.

## Pre-Flight

- [ ] All required inputs collected and accessible
- [ ] Stakeholders identified and availability confirmed
- [ ] Timeline and deadlines established

## Execution Steps

- [ ] Step 1: Define programme objectives and success criteria
- [ ] Step 2: Set partner selection criteria (ICP fit, readiness, sponsorship)
- [ ] Step 3: Recruit 3-8 design partners from pipeline and strategic prospects
- [ ] Step 4: Structure engagement cadence (weekly sessions, monthly exec check-ins)
- [ ] Step 5: Define feedback framework with structured instruments
- [ ] Step 6: Establish value exchange terms
- [ ] Step 7: Run programme and synthesise weekly feedback
- [ ] Step 8: Gate transition to paid customer status
- [ ] Step 9: Capture outcomes: use cases, testimonials, pricing signal
- [ ] Step 10: Retrospect and templatise for future programmes

## Post-Completion

- [ ] All deliverables produced and stored
- [ ] Stakeholders notified of completion
- [ ] Lessons learned captured for process improvement
