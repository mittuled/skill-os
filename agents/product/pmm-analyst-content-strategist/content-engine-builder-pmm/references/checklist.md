# Checklist: content-engine-builder-pmm

Builds and operates the content production engine for ongoing PMM content.

## Pre-Flight

- [ ] All required inputs collected and accessible
- [ ] Stakeholders identified and availability confirmed
- [ ] Timeline and deadlines established

## Execution Steps

- [ ] Step 1: Audit existing content operations and identify bottlenecks
- [ ] Step 2: Define 90-day content calendar tied to product roadmap
- [ ] Step 3: Build templates and style guides for each content type
- [ ] Step 4: Establish review pipeline with roles and SLAs
- [ ] Step 5: Launch first full cycle and measure throughput
- [ ] Step 6: Iterate quarterly based on performance data

## Post-Completion

- [ ] All deliverables produced and stored
- [ ] Stakeholders notified of completion
- [ ] Lessons learned captured for process improvement
