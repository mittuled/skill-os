## Executive Summary

[2-3 sentences summarizing what triggered the adjustment, the selected option, and the impact on the phase.
GUIDANCE: Lead with the decision made and its effect on the delivery timeline.]

## Change Trigger

[Description of what changed, with quantified impact.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: "Lost 30% of engineering capacity for sprints 4-6 due to reassignment of 2 engineers to P0 incident response. Current sprint velocity of 24 SP/sprint drops to estimated 17 SP/sprint. Remaining committed scope requires 42 SP across 2 sprints."
- Bad: "We lost some capacity"
- Format: Paragraph with specific numbers, dates, and root cause]

## Updated Priority Stack

[Re-ranked scope items with MoSCoW classification.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: Table with Item, Original Priority, Updated MoSCoW, Value Justification, Estimate
- Bad: List of features without classification
- Format: Markdown table ordered by updated priority]

## Scope Options

[2-3 scope scenarios with trade-off analysis.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: "Option A (minimal cut): Ship Must + all Should items. Defers Could items (analytics dashboard, CSV export). Exit criteria fully met. Timeline: +1 sprint. Option B (moderate cut): Ship Must + priority Should items. Defers remaining Should + Could. Core exit criteria met. Timeline: on schedule."
- Bad: "We can cut some things"
- Format: One subsection per option with What Ships, What is Deferred, Exit Criteria Impact, Timeline Impact]

## Decision Record

[Selected option with rationale and approver.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: "Decision: Option B selected by VP Product on 2026-03-15. Rationale: On-schedule delivery of core value (RBAC enforcement) outweighs deferring the admin audit log, which is valuable but not required for launch. Deferred items placed in Q3 backlog with capacity confirmed."
- Bad: "We went with option B"
- Format: Decision, Approver, Date, Rationale (2-3 sentences), Deferred Item Landing Zone]

## Recommendations

[Post-adjustment actions.
GUIDANCE: Each recommendation should be:
- Specific (not "monitor scope" but "re-estimate remaining stories in sprint 5 planning with updated velocity of 17 SP/sprint")
- Actionable (assignable to a person/team)
- Prioritized (P1/P2/P3)]
