# Checklist: dependency-resolver

Resolves cross-team dependency conflicts through negotiation, re-sequencing, or escalation.

## Pre-Flight

- [ ] All required inputs collected and accessible
- [ ] Stakeholders identified and availability confirmed
- [ ] Timeline and deadlines established

## Execution Steps

- [ ] Step 1: Review dependency map and identify unresolved blocking items
- [ ] Step 2: Contact owning team PM/tech lead for capacity and delivery date
- [ ] Step 3: Assess resolution options: direct, re-sequence, scope reduction, workaround, escalation
- [ ] Step 4: Select resolution path and document action, owner, committed date, fallback
- [ ] Step 5: Update dependency map with resolution status
- [ ] Step 6: Communicate resolution plan to both teams
- [ ] Step 7: Set midpoint follow-up checkpoint
- [ ] Step 8: Close dependency item when deliverable confirmed complete

## Post-Completion

- [ ] All deliverables produced and stored
- [ ] Stakeholders notified of completion
- [ ] Lessons learned captured for process improvement
