# gtm-activation-pmm

## Agent: PMM
L2 product marketing manager responsible for competitive intelligence, positioning, GTM planning, pricing strategy, launch narrative, and sales enablement.

Department ethos: [ideal-product.md](../../../../departments/product/ideal-product.md)

## Skill Description
Activates the GTM plan at launch including channel execution and partner coordination, turning the strategic launch plan into coordinated, time-bound actions across marketing, sales, CS, and partner teams.

## When to Use
- Launch day or launch-week execution window has arrived and the GTM plan needs to go live.
- A phased rollout requires sequenced activation across segments, tiers, or geographies.
- Partner or co-marketing launch activities need coordinated timing with internal channels.
- A launch has been delayed and activation assets need re-sequencing.
- Post-launch, early signal data suggests activation adjustments (channel mix, messaging emphasis).

## Workflow
1. Confirm launch readiness. Verify that all prerequisite deliverables are complete: enablement brief distributed, sales trained, product deployed, marketing assets staged, and PR/comms queued.
2. Distribute the activation checklist to all functional leads with time-stamped milestones. Each item has an owner, a due time, and a done/blocked status.
3. Trigger owned-channel activation: product changelog, in-app announcement, email campaign to target segment, blog post, and social posts per the content calendar.
4. Coordinate earned and partner channels. Confirm analyst briefing schedule, partner co-marketing posts, and any press embargo lifts are synchronised with owned-channel timing.
5. Activate sales motion. Confirm the enablement brief is pinned in the sales channel, CRM fields are updated for the new product/feature, and SDR sequences are live.
6. Monitor activation metrics in real time during the first 24-48 hours: email open and click rates, blog traffic, in-app engagement, social impressions, and inbound demo requests.
7. Run a daily activation standup during launch week. Surface blockers, adjust channel emphasis based on early signals, and redistribute budget if a channel underperforms.
8. Coordinate any Day 2+ content drops (customer story, demo video, webinar registration push) that were planned as part of a multi-day launch sequence.
9. At the end of launch week, compile an activation summary: what fired on time, what was blocked, early performance metrics, and recommended follow-up actions.
10. Hand off sustained marketing motions (ongoing campaigns, nurture sequences) to the demand-gen or content team with context on what resonated during activation.

## Anti-Patterns
- **Activating channels without confirming product readiness.** *Why: Driving traffic to a feature that is not yet deployed destroys buyer trust and creates support load with zero conversion.*
- **Treating activation as a single-day event.** *Why: Multi-day launch sequences outperform single-day blasts because different buyer segments engage on different timelines.*
- **Skipping the real-time monitoring loop.** *Why: Without early signal data, you cannot redirect spend or emphasis to the channels that are actually working.*
- **Letting each team activate independently without a shared checklist.** *Why: Uncoordinated activation leads to messaging inconsistency, timing gaps, and duplicated efforts.*
- **Failing to hand off sustained motions post-launch.** *Why: Launch energy dissipates within a week; without a handoff, pipeline generation drops off a cliff after activation ends.*

## Output

**Success:**
- All activation checklist items completed within the planned time window with owner sign-off.
- Activation summary report with channel-level performance metrics delivered within 48 hours of launch-week close.
- Clean handoff document for sustained marketing motions with performance context.

**Failure:**
- Channels activated out of sequence, causing messaging confusion or traffic to unready assets.
- No real-time monitoring during launch week, resulting in wasted spend on underperforming channels.
- Activation ends with no summary and no handoff, leaving sustained motions orphaned.
