# Framework: Early Adopter Success

## Core Model

Early adopter success is a structured programme that ensures design partners and beta users achieve their target outcome, generate high-quality product signal, and become advocates — converting early access into a repeatable GTM asset.

## Programme Phases

| Phase | Duration | Primary Goal | Key Activity |
|-------|----------|-------------|-------------|
| Selection | Pre-launch | Recruit qualified cohort | Score and recruit candidates |
| Activation | Days 0–7 | Reach first value moment | Kickoff call + setup support |
| Engagement | Weeks 2–6 | Sustain usage; collect signal | Weekly check-in cadence |
| Graduation | End of beta | Assess readiness; drive referrals | Success review + case study ask |

## Cohort Selection Criteria

Score each candidate on:
- **Strategic value** (0-5): Is this a target ICP account? Would they make a strong case study?
- **Engagement likelihood** (0-5): Have they expressed urgency? Do they have a specific use case?
- **Signal quality** (0-5): Will their usage reflect real-world conditions? Do they have the team size and workflow complexity to stress-test the feature?
- **Risk tolerance** (0-5): Are they comfortable with rough edges and willing to give candid feedback?

Minimum total score for selection: 12/20.

## Success Criteria Definition

Before programme launch, define for each participant:
- **Activation milestone**: First meaningful action completed (measurable, binary)
- **Success milestone**: Outcome achieved that justified joining (observable by Week 4)
- **Signal contribution target**: Minimum feedback sessions, NPS responses, or bug reports expected

## Check-in Cadence

| Cadence | Format | Owner | Trigger to Escalate |
|---------|--------|-------|---------------------|
| Day 3 pulse | Async (email/Slack) | Ops | No response or activation incomplete |
| Week 1 call | 30-min video | CS + Product | Activation blocked or setup stalled |
| Bi-weekly | 30-min video or async | CS | Usage drop or negative signal |
| Exit review | 60-min video | Product | Programme end |

## Graduation Criteria

An early adopter is ready to graduate to GA or become a reference when:
- [ ] Success milestone achieved (documented)
- [ ] Minimum 2 feedback sessions completed
- [ ] No open critical or high-severity bugs blocking their workflow
- [ ] NPS or satisfaction score >= 7/10
- [ ] Willing to participate in case study, testimonial, or reference call (optional but tracked)

## Signal Collection Standards

Feedback must be tagged by:
- **Type**: Bug / UX friction / Missing feature / Positive signal / Churn risk
- **Severity**: Critical (blocking) / High (significant friction) / Medium / Low
- **Frequency**: Is this isolated or have multiple participants raised it?

Never report raw quotes without tag classification — it prevents aggregation and action.
