## Bottleneck Analysis

### 1. Proposal → Closed Won — CRITICAL (16% vs. 30%)

**Volume impact:** 32 proposals → only 5 closed-won. At benchmark (30%), expected ~10 closed-won — the gap is 5 deals per quarter.

**Root cause signals:**
- Competitive loss rate spiked (segment notes)
- Pricing objections increased vs. prior quarter
- Avg days in stage: 28 days vs. 21-day benchmark — deals dragging

**Recommended action:** Conduct win/loss analysis on Q1 losses; review competitive objection handling in sales playbook; consider pricing review for mid-market segment.

---

### 2. SQL → Opportunity — WARNING (48% vs. 55%)

**Segmentation finding:** SDR-sourced opps converting at 38% vs. AE-sourced at 61%. The gap suggests SDRs are advancing deals that don't meet full qualification criteria to hit pipeline targets.

**Volume impact:** At benchmark (55%), expected ~59 opps from 107 SQLs — actual 51 means 8 opps/quarter below target.

**Recommended action:** Improve SDR discovery call training; review disqualification criteria to prevent premature stage advancement.
