# Corporate Governance Framework

Reference framework for drafting bylaws and establishing board governance for newly incorporated entities.

## Delaware General Corporation Law (DGCL) Requirements

### Bylaws (DGCL 109)
- Board or stockholders may adopt, amend, or repeal bylaws
- Certificate of incorporation may vest bylaw amendment power exclusively in the board
- Bylaws may not conflict with the certificate of incorporation or DGCL

### Board of Directors (DGCL 141)
- Business managed by or under the direction of the board
- Number of directors fixed by bylaws (or certificate); minimum 1
- Directors elected at annual stockholder meeting unless certificate provides for classified board
- Board may act by unanimous written consent without a meeting (DGCL 141(f))

### Officers (DGCL 142)
- Officers appointed by the board or as provided in bylaws
- One person may hold multiple offices
- No specific officer titles required by DGCL (but practical necessity: CEO, Secretary, Treasurer)

### Meetings and Quorum (DGCL 141(b))
- Quorum: majority of total directors unless certificate/bylaws specify different (but not less than 1/3)
- Action requires majority vote of directors present at a meeting with quorum
- Meetings may be held via teleconference (DGCL 141(i))

## Bylaws Structure

### Required Provisions
1. **Stockholder Meetings**: Annual and special meeting procedures, notice requirements (10-60 days per DGCL 222), record date, quorum (majority unless otherwise specified)
2. **Board of Directors**: Number, qualifications, term, vacancies, removal (with or without cause), regular and special meeting procedures
3. **Officers**: Titles, duties, appointment, removal, succession
4. **Indemnification**: Mandatory advancement and indemnification provisions (DGCL 145)
5. **Stock**: Certificates or uncertificated shares, transfer restrictions, record holders
6. **Amendments**: Procedure for amending bylaws

### Investor-Driven Provisions
- **Protective provisions**: Require investor consent for specified actions (typically in certificate, cross-referenced in bylaws)
- **Board observer rights**: Non-voting attendance at board meetings per investor agreement
- **Information rights**: Financial reporting cadence and access per NVCA-standard terms
- **Drag-along/tag-along**: Usually in stockholder agreement, not bylaws

## Initial Board Resolutions Checklist

### Organizational Resolutions
- [ ] Adopt bylaws
- [ ] Elect officers (CEO, Secretary, Treasurer at minimum)
- [ ] Designate fiscal year
- [ ] Authorize bank accounts and signatories
- [ ] Adopt equity incentive plan (with stockholder approval)
- [ ] Authorize issuance of founder shares
- [ ] Ratify pre-incorporation actions (contracts, expenses, agreements)
- [ ] Appoint registered agent
- [ ] Authorize qualification as foreign corporation in operating states
- [ ] Adopt Section 83(b) election policy for restricted stock recipients
- [ ] Authorize engagement of accountants and tax advisors
- [ ] Adopt form of indemnification agreement for directors and officers

### Protective Measures
- [ ] Adopt D&O indemnification provisions in bylaws (DGCL 145(a)-(b) permissive; 145(c) mandatory for successful defense)
- [ ] Authorize D&O insurance procurement
- [ ] Adopt advancement of expenses provision (DGCL 145(e))
- [ ] Adopt exculpation provision in certificate (DGCL 102(b)(7) — eliminates personal liability for monetary damages for breach of fiduciary duty of care)

## Governance Best Practices

### Meeting Procedures
- Regular board meetings: quarterly minimum
- Written consent: for routine matters between meetings
- Minutes: prepare and circulate within 7 days of meeting
- Board materials: distribute 3+ business days before meeting

### Fiduciary Duties
- **Duty of Care**: Informed decision-making, reasonable inquiry, reliance on experts (business judgment rule protection)
- **Duty of Loyalty**: No self-dealing, corporate opportunities doctrine, conflicts disclosure
- **Duty of Good Faith**: Act in honest belief that actions are in best interest of corporation (Caremark duties for oversight)
