# 83(b) Election Coordination Checklist

Step-by-step checklist for coordinating IRC Section 83(b) election filings from eligibility determination through record keeping.

## Eligibility Verification

- [ ] Confirm the grant is "property transferred in connection with performance of services" under IRC 83(a)
- [ ] Verify the property is subject to a "substantial risk of forfeiture" (vesting schedule, repurchase rights)
- [ ] Confirm the grant type qualifies: restricted stock (YES), stock options at exercise if unvested (MAYBE — analyze under Reg 1.83-3(a)(2)), RSUs (NO — not property until settled)
- [ ] Determine the grant date (date of transfer under IRC 83) — this starts the 30-day clock
- [ ] Calculate the absolute filing deadline: grant date + 30 calendar days (no extensions, per IRC 83(b)(2))
- [ ] Verify the fair market value at transfer date (per most recent 409A valuation or board-determined FMV)
- [ ] Confirm the amount paid by the recipient for the property (typically par value for restricted stock)

## Election Form Preparation

### Required Information (per Reg 1.83(b)-1(d))
- [ ] Taxpayer's name, address, and Social Security Number
- [ ] Description of the property (number of shares, class, company name)
- [ ] Date of transfer (grant date)
- [ ] Taxable year for which election is made
- [ ] Nature of restrictions on the property (vesting schedule, forfeiture conditions)
- [ ] Fair market value of the property at time of transfer (without restrictions)
- [ ] Amount paid for the property
- [ ] Statement that copies have been furnished to other persons as required

### Document Package
- [ ] IRS filing copy (original, signed)
- [ ] Company copy (signed duplicate)
- [ ] Recipient's personal copy (for attachment to personal tax return)
- [ ] Cover letter to IRS with taxpayer identification
- [ ] Certified mail form (PS Form 3800) prepared

## Filing Execution

- [ ] Election filed with IRS Service Center where recipient files personal returns (per Reg 1.83(b)-1(b))
- [ ] Filed via USPS Certified Mail with Return Receipt Requested (proof of timely filing)
- [ ] Filing date confirmed as postmark date (not receipt date) — must be within 30 days
- [ ] Certified mail tracking number recorded
- [ ] Copy delivered to company (employer) within 30 days of transfer
- [ ] Recipient notified to attach copy to personal tax return for the taxable year of transfer

## Post-Filing Record Keeping

- [ ] Certified mail return receipt (green card) filed when received
- [ ] Election form copies stored in company legal records (corporate minute book)
- [ ] Equity tracking system updated: 83(b) election filed = YES, filing date, receipt tracking number
- [ ] CFO/payroll notified for tax reporting purposes (no income withholding required at grant if 83(b) filed; income recognized at grant date FMV minus amount paid)
- [ ] Cap table notation updated to reflect 83(b) status
- [ ] Reminder set: confirm return receipt within 4-6 weeks of mailing

## Deadline Monitoring System

- [ ] All outstanding restricted stock grants logged with: recipient name, grant date, 30-day deadline date
- [ ] Automated reminders configured:
  - Day 0 (grant date): Initial notification to recipient with election form and instructions
  - Day 15: Reminder to recipient and legal
  - Day 25: Urgent escalation to recipient, legal, and General Counsel
  - Day 28: Final warning — file immediately or escalate
- [ ] Overdue tracking: any missed deadlines documented with explanation and tax impact analysis

## Common Errors to Prevent

- [ ] Do NOT file for stock options that have not been exercised (election is at exercise, not grant)
- [ ] Do NOT file for RSUs (not "property" until settlement — IRC 83 does not apply until shares delivered)
- [ ] Do NOT assume email or fax filing is acceptable (IRS requires mail)
- [ ] Do NOT rely on IRS to confirm receipt (IRS does not send acceptance/rejection — certified mail receipt is the only proof)
- [ ] Do NOT miss the deadline assuming it can be extended (30-day deadline is statutory; no extensions, no exceptions, no relief under Reg 301.9100)
