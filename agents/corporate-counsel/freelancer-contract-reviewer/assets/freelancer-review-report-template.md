## Recommended Modifications

[Specific contract changes recommended, prioritized by risk reduction.

GUIDANCE: Each recommendation should be:
- Specific (include draft replacement language where possible)
- Actionable (identify which contract section to modify)
- Prioritized (P1: must-change before signing / P2: should-change / P3: nice-to-have)

Example:
- P1: Section 4.2 — Remove exclusivity clause entirely, or replace with: "Contractor shall not provide services to direct competitors of Company during the Term, but is otherwise free to engage other clients."
- P1: Section 6.1 — Add background IP carve-out: "Notwithstanding the foregoing, Contractor retains all rights to Contractor's pre-existing intellectual property as listed in Exhibit A."
- P2: Section 3.1 — Replace fixed schedule with: "Contractor shall be available for scheduled coordination meetings but otherwise determines their own work schedule."
- P3: Section 8.3 — Add kill fee provision for early termination without cause.]

## Compliance Checklist

[Post-review compliance verification checklist.

- [ ] Classification risk score within acceptable range (B or above)
- [ ] All high-risk provisions addressed or accepted with documented rationale
- [ ] IRS 20-factor test results reviewed with employment counsel (if score is C or below)
- [ ] ABC test passed (if California engagement)
- [ ] Background IP carve-out included

- [ ] No company-provided email, title, or org chart inclusion
- [ ] Worker free to serve other clients
- [ ] Worker determines own schedule and methods
- [ ] Tax classification (1099) explicitly stated
