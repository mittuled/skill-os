## Business Case for the Customer

Acme Corp will exhaust their 200-seat allocation within 4-6 weeks based on current growth rate. Purchasing 50 additional seats now:
- Avoids productivity disruption when new hires cannot access the platform
- Locks in current per-seat pricing before the upcoming price review
- Qualifies for the annual multi-seat discount (saves ~12% vs month-to-month overage pricing)

## Expansion Proposal

- **Recommended offer**: 50-seat expansion (Growth plan), moving from 200 to 250 seats
- **Estimated ARR uplift**: $28,000 (50 seats × $560/seat/year)
- **Alternative**: Upgrade to Enterprise plan (500 seats) — positions for 12-month growth, includes SSO and priority support
