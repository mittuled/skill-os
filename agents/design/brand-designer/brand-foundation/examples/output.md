## Mission and Vision

**Mission**: Help engineering teams ship software faster without sacrificing quality

**Vision**: A world where software teams spend more time building than managing process

**Brand Promise**: Flowline cuts your deployment cycle in half — or you don't pay.

## Target Audience

Engineering teams at high-growth startups and mid-market companies.

## Brand Values

| Value | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| Speed with intention | Fast is only good if the outcome is right — we move fast and make good decisions |
| Engineering excellence | We are built by engineers, for engineers — craft matters |
| Radical transparency | No black boxes — customers see exactly what the platform is doing and why |
| Team empowerment | Great teams need tools that trust them; we don't add bureaucracy |

| Modern | — |

## Tone of Voice

**Primary tone**: Bold, direct, empowering

### Do
1. Use engineering-native language (PRs, deploys, pipelines)
2. Be specific — quote real numbers from customer data
3. Address engineering leads directly
4. Lead with outcomes, not features

### Don't
1. Use business buzzwords (synergy, leverage, ecosystem)
2. Over-explain concepts that engineers know
3. Sound like a marketing team writing for engineers
4. Make unsubstantiated performance claims
