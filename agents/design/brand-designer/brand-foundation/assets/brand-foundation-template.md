## Mission and Vision

[Document the foundational statements that define why the company exists and where it is going.

GUIDANCE:
- Good mission: "We exist to give every infrastructure engineer the same visibility that used to require a 5-person SRE team."
- Bad mission: "We provide world-class monitoring solutions to enterprise customers." (generic, no why)
- Good vision: "A world where no production incident goes undiagnosed for more than 5 minutes."
- Bad vision: "To be the leading monitoring platform." (outcome, not vision)]
