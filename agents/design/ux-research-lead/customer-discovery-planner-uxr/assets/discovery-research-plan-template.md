## Discussion Guide (Outline)

[Full discussion guide is a separate document — this section outlines the key topics.]

**Warm-up** (5 min):
- Role, company, team size; how long in current role

**Current workflow** (15 min):
- Walk me through how you manage [topic area] today
- What tools do you use? Why those?
- Tell me about the last time you had to [specific behaviour]

**Problem exploration** (20 min):
- What is most frustrating about your current approach?
- When does [process] break down?
- What do you wish you could do that you cannot?

**Decision and priority understanding** (10 min):
- When you are evaluating solutions, what matters most to you?
- Have you tried other approaches? What happened?

**Wrap-up** (5 min):
- Is there anything important we have not covered?
- Thank participant
