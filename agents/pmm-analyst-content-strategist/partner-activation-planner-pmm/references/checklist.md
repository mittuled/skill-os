# Checklist: partner-activation-planner-pmm

Plans partner activation activities for product launches.

## Pre-Flight

- [ ] All required inputs collected and accessible
- [ ] Stakeholders identified and availability confirmed
- [ ] Timeline and deadlines established

## Execution Steps

- [ ] Step 1: Identify activation partners by integration readiness and audience overlap
- [ ] Step 2: Define joint activities (co-branded content, webinars, showcases)
- [ ] Step 3: Produce co-marketing assets approved by both brands
- [ ] Step 4: Execute and track participation and lead attribution

## Post-Completion

- [ ] All deliverables produced and stored
- [ ] Stakeholders notified of completion
- [ ] Lessons learned captured for process improvement
