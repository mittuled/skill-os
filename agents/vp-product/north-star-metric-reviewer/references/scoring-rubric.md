# Scoring Rubric: north-star-metric-reviewer

Reviews whether the north-star metric still reflects product and business goals, recommending recalibration when alignment degrades.

## Criteria

| # | Criterion | Weight | Description |
|---|-----------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Current Metric Documentation | 15% | Completeness of existing metric definition including formula, source, cadence, and original rationale |
| 2 | Strategic Alignment Analysis | 25% | Depth of comparison against current objectives, vision, and ICP definition |
| 3 | Leading-Lagging Correlation | 30% | Quality of analysis showing whether north star movements predict retention, revenue, and satisfaction |
| 4 | Goodhart Risk Evaluation | 15% | Specificity of gaming evidence with concrete examples |
| 5 | Recommendation Quality | 15% | Clarity of reaffirm/revise/composite recommendation with migration plan |
| **Total** | | **100%** | |

## Scale

Each criterion is scored **0-10**:
- **0**: No evidence / completely absent
- **5**: Partially present with significant gaps
- **10**: Fully present, comprehensive, no gaps

**Composite Score** = Σ (criterion score × weight)

## Grade Bands

| Grade | Composite Score | Label | Description | Recommended Action |
|-------|----------------|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| A+ | 9.0 – 10.0 | Exceptional | Exceptional performance across all criteria | Ratify metric for upcoming cycle |
| A | 8.0 – 8.9 | Strong | Strong performance across all criteria | Ratify with minor measurement adjustments |
| B | 7.0 – 7.9 | Good | Good performance across all criteria | Conduct deeper correlation analysis before ratifying |
| C | 5.0 – 6.9 | Adequate | Adequate performance across all criteria | Investigate Goodhart signals before deciding |
| D | 3.0 – 4.9 | Weak | Weak performance across all criteria | Convene leadership to clarify strategic direction before reviewing |
| F | 0.0 – 2.9 | Failing | Failing performance across all criteria | No metric review performed |
