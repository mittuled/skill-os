# Checklist: pricing-v1-setter

Sets initial pricing model and price points for first commercial release.

## Pre-Flight

- [ ] All required inputs collected and accessible
- [ ] Stakeholders identified and availability confirmed
- [ ] Timeline and deadlines established

## Execution Steps

- [ ] Step 1: Establish pricing objectives (land-and-expand vs. revenue max vs. market share)
- [ ] Step 2: Analyse value metrics (seats, API calls, outcomes)
- [ ] Step 3: Research willingness-to-pay (Van Westendorp, conjoint, benchmarks)
- [ ] Step 4: Select pricing model (subscription, usage, hybrid)
- [ ] Step 5: Set price points per tier **[GATE]**
- [ ] Step 6: Model unit economics (3 scenarios)
- [ ] Step 7: Stress-test with GTM stakeholders
- [ ] Step 8: Define discounting guardrails
- [ ] Step 9: Document pricing bible and enable Sales
- [ ] Step 10: Plan 90-day pricing review cadence

## Post-Completion

- [ ] All deliverables produced and stored
- [ ] Stakeholders notified of completion
- [ ] Lessons learned captured for process improvement
