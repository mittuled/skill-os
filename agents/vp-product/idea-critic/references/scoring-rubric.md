# Scoring Rubric: idea-critic

Critiques incoming product ideas to identify flawed assumptions and surface the strongest opportunities.

## Criteria

| # | Criterion | Weight | Description |
|---|-----------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Neutral Restatement | 15% | Accuracy of idea summary without advocacy or dismissal |
| 2 | Assumption Extraction | 25% | Completeness of implicit assumptions identified across user behaviour, market, and feasibility |
| 3 | Assumption Stress Test | 30% | Quality of evidence assessment and risk classification for each assumption |
| 4 | Strategic Alignment | 15% | Accuracy of scoring against company priorities, target market, and resource availability |
| 5 | Verdict Quality | 15% | Clarity and actionability of advance/revise/shelve recommendation |
| **Total** | | **100%** | |

## Scale

Each criterion is scored **0-10**:
- **0**: No evidence / completely absent
- **5**: Partially present with significant gaps
- **10**: Fully present, comprehensive, no gaps

**Composite Score** = Σ (criterion score × weight)

## Grade Bands

| Grade | Composite Score | Label | Description | Recommended Action |
|-------|----------------|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| A+ | 9.0 – 10.0 | Exceptional | Exceptional performance across all criteria | Route idea to opportunity framing |
| A | 8.0 – 8.9 | Strong | Strong performance across all criteria | Route with minor clarifications needed |
| B | 7.0 – 7.9 | Good | Good performance across all criteria | Request specific additional evidence before routing |
| C | 5.0 – 6.9 | Adequate | Adequate performance across all criteria | Send back with structured feedback on gaps |
| D | 3.0 – 4.9 | Weak | Weak performance across all criteria | Redo critique with better source materials |
| F | 0.0 – 2.9 | Failing | Failing performance across all criteria | No meaningful critique performed |
