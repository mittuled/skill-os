# Checklist: GTM Sales Activation Readiness

## Purpose

Verifies that all prerequisites for a sales-side GTM activation are in place before launch. Use at the Sales Readiness Checkpoint step (Step 5 of workflow).

## Pre-Launch Readiness Checklist

### Pipeline Foundation (Must-Have)

- [ ] Revenue target confirmed and approved by VP Sales
- [ ] Pipeline coverage ratio set (typically 3-4x) with historical justification
- [ ] Stage conversion rates calculated from last 2 quarters of CRM data
- [ ] Weekly pipeline generation targets calculated and assigned to reps
- [ ] Pipeline waterfall model reviewed and stress-tested

### Outreach Readiness (Must-Have)

- [ ] Outreach sequences built for each buyer persona (minimum 2 personas)
- [ ] Each sequence has 5+ touches across 2+ channels
- [ ] Messaging aligned to GTM value proposition (cross-checked with PMM)
- [ ] Email templates loaded into outreach tool (Outreach, Salesloft, etc.)
- [ ] Call talk tracks documented and distributed

### Territory and Assignment (Must-Have)

- [ ] Target account list finalized with tier assignments (Tier 1/2/3)
- [ ] Accounts assigned to individual reps with no overlaps
- [ ] High-priority (Tier 1) accounts have named owners confirmed
- [ ] Rep capacity validated (existing pipeline + new activation load)

### Enablement Materials (Must-Have)

- [ ] Talk tracks for each buyer persona reviewed and approved
- [ ] Battle cards for top 3 competitors updated for this launch
- [ ] Demo environment provisioned and tested
- [ ] Pricing one-pager reflects current pricing and packaging
- [ ] Objection handler includes launch-specific objections

### Enablement Delivery (Must-Have)

- [ ] Sales kickoff or enablement session scheduled before launch date
- [ ] All reps confirmed attendance
- [ ] Role-play or practice session scheduled post-enablement

### CRM and Tracking (Should-Have)

- [ ] Campaign tracking configured in CRM (source = activation campaign)
- [ ] Pipeline reports built to track activation-sourced pipeline separately
- [ ] Weekly review cadence scheduled (pipeline review + activation metrics)

### Marketing Alignment (Should-Have)

- [ ] Marketing campaign timeline confirmed and synced with sales activation
- [ ] Inbound lead routing configured for activation segment
- [ ] Shared messaging document reviewed by both Sales and Marketing
- [ ] Joint dashboard for lead-to-pipeline visibility

## Launch-Day Verification

- [ ] All reps have logged into outreach tool and confirmed sequence access
- [ ] First batch of outreach scheduled or sent
- [ ] Pipeline tracking dashboard live and accessible
- [ ] Escalation path defined for first-week issues (enablement gaps, tool problems, messaging questions)

## Post-Launch Review Schedule

| Timing | Review Focus | Owner |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| Day 3 | First outreach metrics (open rates, reply rates) | Sales Manager |
| Week 1 | Meetings booked, initial pipeline created, rep feedback | Sales Manager |
| Week 2 | Pipeline quality (stage advancement), messaging effectiveness | Sales Manager + VP Sales |
| Week 4 | Pipeline vs. target, conversion rates, course corrections needed | VP Sales |
| Week 8 | Full activation assessment, scale or pivot decision | VP Sales + CBO |
