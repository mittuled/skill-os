## Financial Narrative Outline

### 1. Where We Are Today (Traction Proof)
Meridian AI has reached $2.8M ARR, growing at 180% year-over-year with 34 paying customers. Gross margin of 71% is consistent with a scalable SaaS model. Net Revenue Retention of 118% demonstrates that existing customers are expanding their usage — the product is delivering measurable value.

### 2. Where We Are Going (Growth Trajectory)
At current growth rates, Meridian AI will exit the year at approximately $7.8M ARR. This Series A raise of $12.0M is sized to fund 28 months of operations at the current burn rate of $420K/month, extending runway to 14 months and providing a clear path to the next financing milestone.

### 3. Why The Economics Work (Unit Economics)
LTV/CAC of 3.8x with a 16-month payback period confirms that each new customer generates substantial long-term value relative to acquisition cost. At 71% gross margin, contribution margin expansion is achievable as the business scales and CAC amortizes.
