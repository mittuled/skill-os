## Executive Summary

[2-3 sentences stating the company's financial position, growth trajectory, and the core unit economics argument. Lead with the headline ARR and growth rate, then the funding ask.

GUIDANCE: Lead with the most defensible number, not the most impressive. Example of good: "[Company] has grown ARR from $Xm to $Ym over 12 months (Z% YoY) with NRR of A%, demonstrating strong product-market retention, and is raising $B to deploy against a bottoms-up sales plan targeting $C ARR in 24 months." Bad: "We are on track to become a $100M ARR company."]
