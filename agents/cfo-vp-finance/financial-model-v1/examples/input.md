# Scenario: Build Initial Financial Model for Series A SaaS Company

Build the v1 financial model for a B2B SaaS company with the following context:

1. **Current state**: 45 customers, $1.8M ARR, 18 months post-launch
2. **Revenue model**: Subscription SaaS with three tiers ($12K, $24K, $60K ACV), 70/25/5 mix
3. **Sales motion**: Inbound-led with 2 AEs, 120 SQLs/month, 15% close rate, 45-day cycle
4. **Unit economics**: Blended CAC $9.2K, gross margin 76%, NRR 112%
5. **Cost structure**: 22 employees ($285K fully-loaded average), $45K/month non-headcount
6. **Cash position**: $3.2M in bank, burning $180K/month net
7. **Objective**: 24-month model to support Series A fundraise targeting $8M raise at $30-40M pre-money

Model should include: revenue forecast, P&L, cash flow, SaaS metrics dashboard, and 3 scenarios.
