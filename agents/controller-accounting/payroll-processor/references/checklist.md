# Checklist: payroll-processor

Step-by-step checklist for executing each payroll run accurately and on time, including equity compensation events and post-payroll reconciliation.

## Payroll Input Collection (T−5 business days)

- [ ] Confirm pay period dates and payroll submission deadline with the payroll provider
- [ ] Collect new hire data: name, SSN, address, W-4, state withholding form, direct deposit
- [ ] Collect termination data: last day, final pay calculation (accrued PTO per state law), COBRA notice
- [ ] Collect compensation changes: salary adjustments, promotions, effective dates, board approvals
- [ ] Collect bonus and commission approvals: written authorization from CEO/CFO for amounts above $5K
- [ ] Collect equity event notifications from cap table system: option exercises, RSU vesting, ESPP purchases
- [ ] Confirm expense reimbursements are approved and included (non-taxable business expense vs. taxable fringe)
- [ ] Verify benefits deduction changes: open enrollment updates, qualifying life events, 401(k) deferral changes

## Equity Compensation Tax Verification (T−3 business days)

- [ ] For NSO exercises: confirm spread (FMV − exercise price) is included as W-2 ordinary income and subject to FICA
- [ ] For ISO exercises: confirm no W-2 withholding required; flag for AMT reporting if applicable (Form 3921)
- [ ] For RSU vesting: confirm FMV of vested shares is included as W-2 income on vest date; apply supplemental withholding rate
- [ ] For ESPP purchases (disqualifying disposition): confirm spread included as ordinary income
- [ ] Coordinate with equity team to confirm share counts and FMV used for tax calculations
- [ ] Ensure sell-to-cover withholding amount matches the tax withholding calculation

## Payroll Calculation Review (T−2 business days)

- [ ] Review payroll register draft from payroll provider
- [ ] Verify gross pay matches approved compensation for all employees
- [ ] Verify federal tax withholding aligns with W-4 and IRS withholding tables
- [ ] Verify state and local tax withholding is correct for all work locations
- [ ] Verify 401(k) deferrals do not exceed annual IRS limits ($23,000 employee / $69,000 total for 2024)
- [ ] Verify FSA/HSA contributions are within annual limits
- [ ] Verify garnishments (child support, tax levies) are applied correctly per court orders
- [ ] Compare net pay per employee against prior period; flag changes greater than 10% for review
- [ ] Obtain manager approval for all off-cycle payments and amounts above $25K

## Payroll Submission (T−1 business day)

- [ ] Submit payroll to the payroll provider before the processing deadline
- [ ] Confirm direct deposit initiation date and employee pay date
- [ ] Confirm paper check generation for any employees without direct deposit
- [ ] Confirm tax withholding deposit schedule (semi-weekly or monthly based on lookback period)
- [ ] Save submission confirmation and file in payroll records

## Post-Payroll Reconciliation (Pay date + 2 business days)

- [ ] Reconcile GL payroll entries against the payroll register:
  - [ ] Gross wages by department
  - [ ] Employer FICA (Social Security 6.2%, Medicare 1.45%)
  - [ ] Federal and state income tax withheld
  - [ ] Benefits deductions (health, dental, vision, 401k, FSA)
- [ ] Verify federal tax deposit was made accurately and timely (Form 941 schedule)
- [ ] Verify state payroll tax deposits were made in all applicable states
- [ ] File state new-hire reports for any new employees within required window (typically 20 days)
- [ ] Update headcount report with new hires and terminations
- [ ] Archive payroll register and reconciliation workpaper in secure records system

## Year-End Payroll Tasks (December/January)

- [ ] Confirm all W-2 information is accurate before the January 31 deadline
- [ ] Confirm employer W-2 copies are distributed to employees
- [ ] File W-2s with the Social Security Administration (Form W-3)
- [ ] File 1099s for any independent contractors (Form 1099-NEC, due January 31)
- [ ] Reconcile annual payroll totals against Form 941 filings
- [ ] Confirm 401(k) Form 5500 deadline (July 31 or October 15 with extension)
