# Checklist: Media Relationship Builder

Operational checklists for auditing existing relationships, building a new media list, and preparing for announcement outreach.

## Checklist 1: Initial Media List Build

Use when building a media list from scratch for a new market or category.

### Research Phase
- [ ] Beat categories defined for the company's category and 2–3 adjacent markets (see framework.md Beat Mapping)
- [ ] Minimum 5 journalists identified per Tier 1 beat category
- [ ] Minimum 10 journalists identified per Tier 2 beat category
- [ ] Each journalist's recent 20 articles reviewed and beat alignment confirmed
- [ ] Preferred contact method identified for each Tier 1 journalist
- [ ] LinkedIn and Twitter/X reviewed for stated interests and recent activity
- [ ] Competitor coverage reviewed — identify which journalists write favourably about competitors (potential opportunity)

### List Validation
- [ ] All email addresses verified (test delivery or use verification tool)
- [ ] No journalists included whose beat has shifted away from the category in the last 6 months
- [ ] No journalists included who have publicly stated they dislike PR pitches for this type of news
- [ ] Tier assignment reviewed by second team member for Tier 1 assignments
- [ ] Initial list shared with relevant executives for input on key relationships they have personally

### CRM Setup
- [ ] All journalists entered in CRM/tracking sheet with required fields (see framework.md CRM Best Practices)
- [ ] Coverage history linked for any journalists who have previously covered the company
- [ ] Tier assignment recorded
- [ ] Follow-up reminder set for first non-transactional touchpoint within 30 days

## Checklist 2: Relationship Audit

Use quarterly to assess the health of existing media relationships.

### Coverage Analysis
- [ ] Last 90 days of coverage reviewed for each Tier 1 and Tier 2 journalist
- [ ] Response rate calculated per journalist for the last 4 pitches
- [ ] Journalists with 0% response rate over 90 days flagged for re-evaluation
- [ ] Tier downgrades actioned for journalists who have shifted beats away from the category
- [ ] New journalists promoted to Tier 2 if coverage shows sustained category focus

### Touchpoint Review
- [ ] CRM last-contact dates reviewed for all Tier 1 and Tier 2 contacts
- [ ] Any Tier 1 contact with last touchpoint >60 days flagged for re-engagement
- [ ] Any Tier 2 contact with last touchpoint >90 days flagged for re-engagement
- [ ] Non-transactional touchpoints confirm: at least 1 value-add contact per Tier 1 journalist per quarter
- [ ] Exclusive and embargo outcomes reviewed — which outlets published, which declined

### Opportunity Gaps
- [ ] Tier-1 outlets with no warm contact identified and listed for investment
- [ ] New journalists covering the category since last audit added to list
- [ ] Beat shifts at key outlets noted and list updated accordingly
- [ ] Agency support evaluated if Tier 1 coverage gaps persist after 2 audit cycles

## Checklist 3: Pre-Announcement Outreach Prep

Use 2 weeks before any major announcement requiring media coverage.

### List Selection
- [ ] Announcement type mapped to relevant beats (product = tech beat; funding = business/startup beat)
- [ ] Tier 1 contacts shortlisted for exclusive or early briefing offer
- [ ] Tier 2 contacts confirmed for embargo outreach
- [ ] Tier 3 contacts listed for post-embargo press release distribution only
- [ ] Internal stakeholders (CEO, CPO, etc.) informed of which journalists are receiving early access

### Pitch Preparation
- [ ] Personalised pitch drafted for each Tier 1 contact — not a copy of the press release
- [ ] Exclusive angle identified and reserved for the single highest-priority Tier 1 contact
- [ ] Embargo terms documented: lift date, lift time, timezone
- [ ] Supporting assets prepared: press release, images, data sheet, spokesperson availability calendar
- [ ] Spokesperson confirmed and briefed on journalist background (see framework.md Beat Research Protocol)

### Outreach Execution
- [ ] Exclusive offer sent to top Tier 1 contact first; await response before offering to next
- [ ] Embargo pitches sent 48–72 hours before desired publication date
- [ ] Embargo acceptance confirmed from each embargoed contact
- [ ] All outreach logged in CRM with date, channel, and response
- [ ] Follow-up cadence set: 1 follow-up maximum at 24 hours post-pitch for non-respondents
- [ ] Coverage tracking enabled in monitoring tool for announcement keywords
