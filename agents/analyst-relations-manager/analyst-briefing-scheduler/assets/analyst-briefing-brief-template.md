## Executive Summary
[2-3 sentences: the briefing objective, the analyst's current perception of the company, and the one narrative shift this briefing is designed to achieve. GUIDANCE: Be specific about the "before state" and "after state." Example: "This briefing prepares [Analyst Name] for the upcoming Gartner Magic Quadrant evaluation cycle opening Q3 2025. The analyst currently positions us as a niche player with limited enterprise traction. This briefing is designed to reframe our Ability to Execute score by presenting three new tier-1 enterprise customer wins and a 94% renewal rate."]
