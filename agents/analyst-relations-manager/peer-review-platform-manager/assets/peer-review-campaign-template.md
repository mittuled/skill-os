## 5. Review Response Protocol

### Positive Reviews (4–5 stars)
- Respond within 48 hours
- Thank reviewer by first name
- Reinforce a specific feature or outcome they mentioned
- Keep response under 80 words

### Neutral Reviews (3 stars)
- Respond within 24 hours
- Acknowledge the feedback
- Offer a follow-up conversation; provide CSM contact
- Never make promises in public response

### Critical Reviews (1–2 stars)
- Respond within 12 hours
- Do NOT be defensive
- Acknowledge; apologize for experience; escalate to support lead
- Take conversation offline immediately
- Flag to CX and Product for trend analysis

## 6. Compliance & Ethical Guidelines

- [ ] No incentivization of positive reviews (violates platform TOS and FTC guidelines)
- [ ] No solicitation targeting competitors' customers with misleading messaging
- [ ] Do NOT request reviews from employees, friends, or family
- [ ] All review requests must include opt-out / unsubscribe option
- [ ] Gartner Peer Insights: only verified customers who have paid for the product
- [ ] G2: each reviewer must have a verified LinkedIn profile or business email
