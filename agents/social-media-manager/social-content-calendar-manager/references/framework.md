# Framework: social-content-calendar-manager

This framework defines platform-specific publishing standards, content mix ratios, and approval workflows for managing a B2B social content calendar.

## Platform-Specific Format Standards

| Platform | Optimal Caption Length | Best Content Formats | Peak Posting Times (US ET) | Max Posts/Week |
|----------|----------------------|---------------------|--------------------------|----------------|
| LinkedIn | 150–300 chars (preview); up to 3,000 if long-form | Text posts, carousels, articles, native video | Tue–Thu, 7–9 AM or 12–2 PM | 3–5 |
| Twitter/X | ≤ 280 chars (thread for long-form) | Text threads, images, short video, polls | Mon–Fri, 8–10 AM or 6–9 PM | 5–10 |
| Instagram | 125 chars (preview); up to 2,200 if needed | Reels (priority), carousels, Stories, static | Mon/Wed/Fri, 11 AM–1 PM | 3–5 feed + daily Stories |
| YouTube | N/A — SEO-optimised title + description | Long-form video (10–20 min), Shorts | Thu–Sat, 12–3 PM | 1–2 |
| TikTok | ≤ 150 chars + hashtags | Short-form video (15–60 sec), duets, trends | Tue/Thu/Fri, 9 AM–12 PM | 3–5 |

## Content Mix Ratios

**3-2-1 Framework** (default for B2B accounts):

| Content Type | Ratio | Examples |
|-------------|-------|---------|
| Educational | 3/6 | How-to guides, industry insights, frameworks, data visualisations, myth-busting |
| Promotional | 2/6 | Product launches, feature announcements, case study links, demo CTAs |
| Community/Personal | 1/6 | Behind-the-scenes, team spotlights, thought leadership opinions, UGC amplification |

**Adjustment signals:**
- If engagement rate drops below platform benchmark: shift ratio toward 4-1-1 (more educational)
- During a product launch sprint: shift to 1-4-1 for 2-week window, then revert
- If follower growth is stagnating: increase community/personal to 2/6 to improve algorithm reach

## Content Calendar Architecture

| Layer | Horizon | Contents |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Strategic themes | Quarterly | 3–5 themes aligned to product roadmap, seasonal moments, campaign pillars |
| Campaign slots | Monthly | Product launches, events, partner activations, content series |
| Post schedule | 2-week rolling | Individual posts with copy, creative brief, UTM, platform, owner, publish time |
| Flex reserve | Weekly | 20% buffer slots for reactive content, trending topics, urgent business needs |

## Approval Workflow by Post Type

| Post Type | Reviewers Required | SLA |
|-----------|-------------------|-----|
| Standard organic post | Social Manager only | No external review needed |
| Product announcement | Product Marketing | 48-hour review window |
| Competitive claim | PMM + Legal | 72-hour review window |
| Executive post (ghostwritten) | Executive + Marketing Lead | 48-hour review window |
| Crisis or sensitive topic | Marketing Lead + PR | Same-day review, escalate immediately |
| Paid amplification of organic | Demand Gen + Finance | 48-hour approval |

## Content Calendar Review Gates

| Gate | Trigger | Action |
|------|---------|--------|
| Weekly performance check | Every Monday | Review prior week; swap underperforming scheduled posts |
| 4-week lookahead review | First Monday of each month | Validate relevance of upcoming posts; update for product/market changes |
| Quarterly content audit | Quarter end | Analyse content mix performance; adjust ratios for next quarter |
| Real-time monitoring | During any major launch or crisis | Monitor hourly; escalate anomalies within 2 hours |
