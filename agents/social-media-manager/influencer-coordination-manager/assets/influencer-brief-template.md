## Content Review and Approval Process

1. Influencer submits draft to brand contact via [email / shared folder / platform DM]
2. Brand reviews within **[3] business days** and provides feedback
3. Influencer incorporates feedback and resubmits within **[2] business days**
4. Final approval sent in writing by brand contact
5. Influencer publishes on agreed date and sends confirmation with post URL

**Maximum revision rounds**: [2]. If alignment is not reached after 2 rounds, escalate to [Name, Title].
