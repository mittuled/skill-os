# Framework: Partner Activation Planning

## Purpose

Provides the structured methodology for evaluating partner fit, designing activation programmes, and setting measurable milestones for partnership success.

## Partner Fit Assessment Method

### Scoring Dimensions

| Dimension | Weight | What to Assess | Data Sources |
|-----------|--------|----------------|-------------|
| Customer Overlap | 30% | Percentage of partner's customer base that matches our ICP; shared accounts identified | CRM cross-reference, partner's public customer list, market data |
| Technical Complementarity | 25% | Integration value (our product + theirs solves a bigger problem); API compatibility; implementation complexity | Technical review with Engineering, partner's documentation |
| GTM Alignment | 25% | Shared sales motion (both sell to same buyer persona); compatible sales cycle; geographic overlap | Partner's sales process review, ICP comparison |
| Strategic Value | 20% | Market access (opens new segments), credibility (brand association), ecosystem position (platform vs. point solution) | Market analysis, brand assessment, ecosystem mapping |

### Scoring Scale

| Score | Label | Criteria |
|-------|-------|----------|
| 5 | Exceptional | Top-tier alignment; immediate revenue opportunity; minimal effort to activate |
| 4 | Strong | Clear alignment; revenue opportunity within 90 days; moderate activation effort |
| 3 | Good | Partial alignment; revenue opportunity within 180 days; significant activation investment |
| 2 | Weak | Limited alignment; uncertain revenue timeline; high activation investment |
| 1 | Poor | Minimal alignment; no clear path to revenue; not worth investment |

### Fit Tiers

| Composite Score | Tier | Investment Level |
|----------------|------|-----------------|
| 4.0 - 5.0 | Tier 1 | Full activation (dedicated BD resource, joint marketing budget, executive sponsorship) |
| 3.0 - 3.9 | Tier 2 | Moderate activation (shared BD resource, co-marketing only, quarterly review) |
| 2.0 - 2.9 | Tier 3 | Light activation (referral agreement, no dedicated resource, semi-annual review) |
| Below 2.0 | No-Go | Do not activate; revisit if conditions change |

## Programme Type Selection

| Programme Type | Best When | Revenue Model | Typical Activation Time |
|---------------|-----------|---------------|------------------------|
| Co-Sell | Shared ICP, complementary products, both have direct sales teams | Revenue share (10-20%) or referral fee ($X per closed deal) | 60-90 days |
| Co-Market | Overlapping audience, content synergies, event opportunities | Lead sharing (no direct revenue; pipeline attribution) | 30-60 days |
| Technology Integration | Products integrate to create joint value; marketplace opportunity | Marketplace listing fee or integration certification fee | 90-180 days (integration build) |
| Channel/Reseller | Partner has distribution in segments we cannot reach directly | Reseller margin (20-40%) | 90-120 days (training + certification) |

## Milestone Setting Method

### 90-Day Gate Structure

| Period | Focus | Key Metrics | Success Threshold |
|--------|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| Days 1-30 | Onboarding and enablement | Partner reps certified, materials delivered | 100% onboarding complete |
| Days 31-60 | First joint activities | Leads generated, meetings booked | 50% of quarterly lead target |
| Days 61-90 | Pipeline creation | Pipeline value created, deals in qualification+ | 30% of quarterly pipeline target |

### Quarterly Targets (After Initial 90 Days)

| Quarter | Pipeline Target | Revenue Target | Activity Targets |
|---------|----------------|----------------|-----------------|
| Q1 (activation) | $X pipeline sourced + influenced | None (building) | N co-marketing activities, N joint calls |
| Q2 | 2x Q1 pipeline | First revenue (closed deals) | N referrals, N co-sell engagements |
| Q3 | Steady-state pipeline | Revenue target based on Q2 close rate | Expanded joint activities |
| Q4 | Scale or optimize | Revenue target based on mature partnership | Annual review and restructure decision |

## Enablement Planning Standards

### What We Deliver to the Partner

| Item | Owner | Timeline | Quality Bar |
|------|-------|----------|-------------|
| ICP and persona training deck | BD + Marketing | Week 1 | Partner can identify our ICP without assistance |
| Product demo training | BD + SE | Week 2 | Partner can deliver a 15-minute standalone demo |
| Joint battle card | BD + Sales | Week 2 | Partner knows how to position us vs. top 3 alternatives |
| Co-branded content (1 piece) | Marketing | Week 3 | Published and distributable |
| Demo environment access | Engineering | Week 1 | Stable, partner-branded, sample data loaded |

### What the Partner Delivers to Us

| Item | Owner | Timeline | Quality Bar |
|------|-------|----------|-------------|
| Customer list overlap analysis | Partner BD | Week 1 | Named accounts with contact details |
| Sales process overview | Partner Sales | Week 1 | We understand how to sell alongside their reps |
| Technical documentation | Partner Engineering | Week 2 | Integration requirements clear |
| Partnership owner designation | Partner Leadership | Day 1 | Named person with authority to execute |

## Governance Design

### Meeting Cadence

| Meeting | Frequency | Attendees | Agenda |
|---------|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Pipeline sync | Weekly | BD + Partner BD | Pipeline review, deal updates, blocker resolution |
| Business review | Monthly | BD + Sales Manager + Partner Sales Lead | KPI review, activity planning, escalation items |
| Executive check-in | Quarterly | VP Sales + Partner VP | Strategic alignment, investment review, scale decisions |

### Escalation Path

| Level | When to Escalate | Owner | SLA |
|-------|-----------------|-------|-----|
| L1: Operational | Day-to-day blockers (lead routing, tool access, scheduling) | BD Manager | 48 hours |
| L2: Strategic | KPI underperformance, partner rep disengagement, integration delays | VP Sales | 1 week |
| L3: Executive | Partnership viability in question, contract disputes, restructure needed | CBO | 2 weeks |
