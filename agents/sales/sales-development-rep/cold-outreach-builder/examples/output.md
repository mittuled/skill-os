## Framework Selection

**PAS — Problem → Agitate → Solve**

Chosen because Sarah is problem-aware: she knows APAC compliance at scale is challenging but has not actively evaluated solutions. PAS surfaces the specific pain (APAC compliance gap), amplifies it with consequences (6-week delays, regulatory exposure), and positions the solution without pressure. AIDA would be too broad for a VP who already understands the space; BAB would be premature without an established solution context.
