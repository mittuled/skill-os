# Scoring Rubric: pricing-legal-reviewer

Evaluates the completeness of a pricing legal review covering antitrust analysis, consumer protection compliance, and recommendation quality.

## Criteria

| # | Criterion | Weight | Description |
|---|-----------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Antitrust Analysis | 35% | Depth of analysis covering predatory pricing, price-fixing risk, tying arrangements, and price discrimination |
| 2 | Consumer Protection Compliance | 35% | Thoroughness of billing practice review including auto-renewal, disclosures, refund policies, and international requirements |
| 3 | Recommendation Quality | 30% | Clarity and actionability of the compliant/modify/reject recommendation with specific alternative structures |

| **Total** | | **100%** | |

## Scale

Each criterion is scored **0-10**:
- **0**: No evidence / completely absent
- **5**: Partially present with significant gaps
- **10**: Fully present, comprehensive, no gaps

**Composite Score** = Σ (criterion score × weight)

## Grade Bands

| Grade | Composite Score | Label | Description | Recommended Action |
|-------|----------------|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| A+ | 9.0 – 10.0 | Exceptional | Full antitrust analysis, all consumer protection items verified, clear recommendation with approved terms | Approve pricing |
| A | 8.0 – 8.9 | Strong | Antitrust analysis thorough, consumer protection mostly verified, recommendation clear | Approve with minor billing adjustments |
| B | 7.0 – 7.9 | Good | Major antitrust concerns addressed, consumer protection partially verified | Approve with conditions: complete consumer protection review |
| C | 5.0 – 6.9 | Adequate | Antitrust analysis surface-level, consumer protection gaps in international compliance | Hold: address gaps before pricing launch |
| D | 3.0 – 4.9 | Weak | Significant antitrust or consumer protection gaps | Block: major review revision needed |
| F | 0.0 – 2.9 | Failing | No pricing legal review performed | Block: full review required |

## Signal Tables

### Antitrust Analysis

| Score | Evidence |
|-------|----------|
| 9-10 | Predatory pricing assessed against market position, Robinson-Patman Act analysis for differential pricing, tying/bundling reviewed for market power abuse, price-fixing risk evaluated (market data coordination), international antitrust (EU competition law) considered |
| 7-8 | Major antitrust dimensions covered (predatory pricing, discrimination, tying), US and primary international markets analysed |
| 5-6 | Core antitrust issues identified but analysis lacks market-specific depth |
| 3-4 | Generic antitrust awareness without specific pricing model analysis |
| 0-2 | No antitrust analysis performed |

### Consumer Protection Compliance

| Score | Evidence |
|-------|----------|
| 9-10 | Auto-renewal compliance verified (California ARL, FTC Negative Option Rule, state-by-state analysis), transparent pricing disclosures confirmed, refund/cancellation policies reviewed, currency conversion practices assessed, drip pricing evaluated, free trial conversion flow reviewed for dark patterns |
| 7-8 | Auto-renewal and disclosure compliance verified for major jurisdictions, refund policies reviewed |
| 5-6 | US consumer protection basics covered, international requirements partially assessed |
| 3-4 | Some consumer protection items checked without systematic coverage |
| 0-2 | No consumer protection review performed |

### Recommendation Quality

| Score | Evidence |
|-------|----------|
| 9-10 | Clear compliant/modify/reject recommendation with scope and assumptions documented. For modifications, specific compliant alternative pricing structures provided. Approved terms documented for implementation. |
| 7-8 | Clear recommendation with alternatives for most flagged items |
| 5-6 | Recommendation provided but alternatives lack specificity |
| 3-4 | Generic recommendation without actionable alternatives |
| 0-2 | No recommendation provided |
