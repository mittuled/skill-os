# Regulatory Compliance Checklist: Terms of Service

This checklist covers regulatory requirements that a Terms of Service must address, organized by regulation and product type.

## GDPR Requirements (24 Items)

Applicable when users are located in the EU/EEA or when processing data of EU residents.

| # | Requirement | GDPR Reference | What to Include |
|---|------------|----------------|-----------------|
| G1 | Identity of data controller | Art. 13(1)(a) | Full legal name and contact details |
| G2 | DPO contact details (if applicable) | Art. 13(1)(b) | Email or postal address of DPO |
| G3 | Purpose of processing | Art. 13(1)(c) | Specific, explicit purposes for each data type |
| G4 | Legal basis for processing | Art. 13(1)(c) | Consent, contract, legitimate interest, etc. per purpose |
| G5 | Legitimate interest description | Art. 13(1)(d) | Specific interest pursued, if legitimate interest is the basis |
| G6 | Recipients or categories of recipients | Art. 13(1)(e) | Who data is shared with (processors, third parties) |
| G7 | Cross-border transfer details | Art. 13(1)(f) | Transfer mechanisms (SCCs, adequacy decisions, BCRs) |
| G8 | Retention periods | Art. 13(2)(a) | Specific periods or criteria for determining retention |
| G9 | Right of access | Art. 13(2)(b), Art. 15 | How to request a copy of personal data |
| G10 | Right to rectification | Art. 13(2)(b), Art. 16 | How to correct inaccurate data |
| G11 | Right to erasure | Art. 13(2)(b), Art. 17 | How to request deletion and applicable exceptions |
| G12 | Right to restrict processing | Art. 13(2)(b), Art. 18 | When and how processing can be restricted |
| G13 | Right to data portability | Art. 13(2)(b), Art. 20 | Machine-readable format, direct transfer where feasible |
| G14 | Right to object | Art. 13(2)(b), Art. 21 | Object to processing based on legitimate interest |
| G15 | Right to withdraw consent | Art. 13(2)(c) | Easy mechanism, no detriment for withdrawal |
| G16 | Right to lodge complaint with supervisory authority | Art. 13(2)(d) | Name and contact of relevant DPA |
| G17 | Whether data provision is statutory/contractual | Art. 13(2)(e) | Consequences of not providing data |
| G18 | Automated decision-making disclosure | Art. 13(2)(f) | Logic, significance, and consequences of profiling |
| G19 | Data protection by design | Art. 25 | Demonstrate privacy-by-design principles |
| G20 | Data processor agreements | Art. 28 | Subprocessor list or reference to processor page |
| G21 | Security measures | Art. 32 | Technical and organizational measures (appropriate level of detail) |
| G22 | Breach notification commitment | Art. 33-34 | Commitment to notify within 72 hours (DPA) and without undue delay (users) |
| G23 | DPIA for high-risk processing | Art. 35 | Disclosure that DPIA was conducted if applicable |
| G24 | International transfer safeguards | Art. 44-49 | Specific transfer mechanism for each destination country |

## CCPA/CPRA Requirements (15 Items)

Applicable when California residents use the product.

| # | Requirement | CCPA Reference | What to Include |
|---|------------|----------------|-----------------|
| C1 | Categories of PI collected | §1798.100(b) | Enumerated categories per CCPA definitions |
| C2 | Purpose for each category | §1798.100(b) | Business or commercial purpose per category |
| C3 | Categories of sources | §1798.110(c)(1) | Where PI is collected from |
| C4 | Business purpose for collection | §1798.110(c)(2) | Specific business purposes |
| C5 | Categories of third parties shared with | §1798.110(c)(3) | By category of PI and recipient type |
| C6 | Right to know | §1798.100, §1798.110 | How to submit access request |
| C7 | Right to delete | §1798.105 | How to submit deletion request and exceptions |
| C8 | Right to opt-out of sale/sharing | §1798.120 | "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link |
| C9 | Right to non-discrimination | §1798.125 | Statement that exercising rights will not result in different treatment |
| C10 | Verification process for requests | §1798.140 | How identity is verified for access/deletion |
| C11 | Authorized agent process | §1798.185 | How consumers can designate an authorized agent |
| C12 | Financial incentive disclosure | §1798.125(b) | Description of any price/service difference for data practices |
| C13 | Sensitive personal information handling | CPRA §1798.121 | Right to limit use of sensitive PI |
| C14 | Retention periods per category | CPRA §1798.100(a)(3) | How long each category is retained |
| C15 | Annual update of privacy disclosures | §1798.130(a)(6) | Commitment to review and update at least annually |

## CAN-SPAM Requirements (8 Items)

Applicable when the product sends commercial email.

| # | Requirement | What to Include |
|---|------------|-----------------|
| CS1 | Accurate header information | Sender identity not misleading |
| CS2 | Non-deceptive subject lines | Subject reflects content |
| CS3 | Advertisement identification | Commercial messages identified as ads |
| CS4 | Physical postal address | Valid physical address in every commercial email |
| CS5 | Opt-out mechanism | Clear, conspicuous unsubscribe option |
| CS6 | Opt-out processing within 10 business days | Commitment to honor opt-outs promptly |
| CS7 | No opt-out requirement charges | Unsubscribe must be free |
| CS8 | Responsibility for third-party senders | Company responsible for messages sent on its behalf |

## COPPA Requirements (10 Items)

Applicable when the product is directed at children under 13 or knowingly collects their data.

| # | Requirement | What to Include |
|---|------------|-----------------|
| CO1 | Parental consent mechanism | Verifiable parental consent before collection |
| CO2 | Direct notice to parents | Description of data practices in parent-directed notice |
| CO3 | Limited collection principle | Collect only what is necessary for the activity |
| CO4 | Parental right to review | Parents can review child's data |
| CO5 | Parental right to revoke consent | Parents can revoke and request deletion |
| CO6 | Operator contact information | Name, address, phone, email of operator |
| CO7 | Description of data collected | Types of information collected from children |
| CO8 | Description of data use | How children's data is used |
| CO9 | Disclosure practices | Whether data is shared with third parties |
| CO10 | Prohibition on conditioning participation | Cannot require more data than necessary to participate |

## Product-Type Clause Sets

### SaaS (30 Clauses)

| # | Clause | Purpose |
|---|--------|---------|
| SA1 | Service description | Define what the service does and does not include |
| SA2 | Account registration requirements | Age, accuracy, single-account rules |
| SA3 | Account security responsibility | User's obligation to protect credentials |
| SA4 | Subscription plans and features | What is included in each tier |
| SA5 | Free trial terms | Duration, conversion, feature limitations |
| SA6 | Payment terms | Billing cycle, currency, payment methods |
| SA7 | Auto-renewal and cancellation | How recurring billing works and how to cancel |
| SA8 | Refund policy | When refunds are available and process |
| SA9 | Price change notice | How and when prices may change |
| SA10 | Service availability (SLA reference) | Uptime commitment or disclaimer |
| SA11 | Data ownership | User retains ownership of their data |
| SA12 | Data portability | Export functionality and supported formats |
| SA13 | Data backup disclaimer | Responsibility for data backup |
| SA14 | License grant to user | Scope of rights to use the service |
| SA15 | License grant from user | Rights to user content for service operation |
| SA16 | Acceptable use policy | Prohibited activities and content |
| SA17 | API terms (if applicable) | Rate limits, attribution, commercial use |
| SA18 | Third-party integrations disclaimer | Liability for connected services |

| # | Clause | Purpose |
|---|--------|---------|

### Social Platform (28 Clauses)

Includes applicable SaaS clauses plus:

| # | Clause | Purpose |
|---|--------|---------|
| SO1 | User-generated content license | Broad license for display, distribution, promotion |
| SO2 | Content moderation rights | Right to remove content, process for appeals |
| SO3 | DMCA/copyright takedown process | Notice and counter-notice procedures |
