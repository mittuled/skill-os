## Account Terms

[User relationship establishment.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: "Eligibility: Users must be 16+ (18+ for paid features). Account creation requires email verification. Users are responsible for all activity under their credentials. Company may suspend accounts for ToS violations with 7-day cure period for non-egregious violations."
- Bad: "You need an account to use the service"
- Format: Clear provisions for eligibility, creation, responsibility, and termination]

## Acceptable Use Policy

[Permitted and prohibited conduct.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: Tiered prohibited activities with consequences: Tier 1 (immediate termination: illegal activity, CSAM, threats), Tier 2 (warning + 7-day cure: spam, impersonation, harassment), Tier 3 (notice: minor policy violations)
- Bad: "Don't do anything bad"
- Format: Enumerated prohibited activities with consequence tiers, linked to content moderation policy]

## Intellectual Property

[Ownership and licence terms.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: "Company retains all IP in the service. Users grant Company a non-exclusive, worldwide licence to host and display user content for service operation. Users retain ownership of their content. DMCA agent designated at [email]."
- Bad: "We own everything"
- Format: Separate provisions for company IP, user content licence, and DMCA process]

## Payment and Billing Terms

[Commercial terms.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: "Subscription billed [monthly/annually] on the anniversary of signup. Auto-renewal with 7-day advance email notice per California ARL Section 17602. Cancel anytime via account settings — effective at period end. Refund: pro-rata for annual plans cancelled within 30 days."
- Bad: "You pay us money"
- Format: Provisions covering pricing, billing, auto-renewal (with statutory compliance), cancellation, and refunds]

## Limitation of Liability

[Liability caps and disclaimers.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: "Service provided 'as is'. Liability capped at fees paid in prior 12 months. Excludes consequential, incidental, and punitive damages. Carve-outs: liability cap does not apply to data breach, wilful misconduct, or IP infringement."
- Bad: "We aren't responsible for anything"
- Format: Warranty disclaimer, liability cap, excluded damages, carve-outs, consumer law savings clause]

## Dispute Resolution

[Resolution mechanisms.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: "Governing law: Delaware. Mandatory binding arbitration (AAA Commercial Rules) for disputes >$10K. Small claims court available for disputes <$10K. 30-day informal resolution period before arbitration. Class action waiver. EU consumers: mandatory consumer protection laws apply regardless."
- Bad: "We'll figure it out"
- Format: Governing law, arbitration terms, class action waiver, small claims carve-out, consumer protection savings]
