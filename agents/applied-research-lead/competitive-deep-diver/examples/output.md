## Executive Summary

RivalCo has raised $40M Series B and expanded directly into our core code review market. Their entry price is 30% below ours. However, they lack AI features, SOC 2 certification, and enterprise support SLAs — our three strongest differentiators. The threat is real but manageable if we communicate these gaps clearly in sales cycles.

## Win/Loss Conditions

**We win when**:
- Deals where AI review is valued by engineering leads
- Enterprise compliance requirements (SOC 2) are on the checklist
- Customer needs multi-cloud deployments

**We lose when**:
- Pure price competition with no compliance or AI requirements
- SMB deals where SOC 2 is not required

## Strategic Implications

1. Competitor is cheaper — review our pricing strategy and value-based differentiation
2. Competitor gaps we can exploit: AI-assisted review, multi-cloud support, SOC 2 certification
3. Monitor recent platform expansion (code review launch) for signals of further strategic direction shift
