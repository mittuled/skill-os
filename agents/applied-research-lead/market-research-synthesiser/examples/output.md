## High Confidence Findings (from customer interviews)

1. Engineers drive tool evaluation; procurement gets involved only after technical approval
2. Free trial is mandatory — no team will purchase without hands-on evaluation
3. Integration with existing GitHub/GitLab workflow is a hard requirement

## Medium Confidence Findings (survey + analyst)

1. 67% of respondents cite "easy onboarding" as top evaluation criterion
2. Price is ranked 4th, below features, integrations, and support quality
3. 42% of teams evaluate 2-3 tools before purchasing
4. PLG motions dominate developer tool adoption in companies under 500 engineers
5. Enterprise deals (1000+ engineers) require security documentation and legal review
