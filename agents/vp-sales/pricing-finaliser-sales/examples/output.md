## Field Feedback Analysis

**Objection frequency:** 40% of deals include a pricing objection in discovery or evaluation
**Average discount depth:** 25% — exceeds the 20% guardrail, indicating list price is not holding

Key field themes:
- AEs report mid-market buyers anchor on CompetitorB's $12,000 street price and use it as leverage
- Champions struggle to build internal ROI justification above $15,000 without VP Sales support
- SDR-sourced deals show higher price resistance than inbound; outbound motion needs updated pricing narrative

**Risk flag:** Discount depth of 25% is structural, not tactical. Reps are not over-discounting — the list price anchor is too high for the mid-market ICP.

## Sign-Off Decision

**Status: CONDITIONAL APPROVAL**

Required adjustments before launch:
1. Reduce Professional tier list price to **$15,500** (aligns to field-validated market clearing price)
2. Set AE discount authority at **10%** (floor = $13,950, preserving current ASP)
3. Create a **mid-market bridge package** at $12,500 for CompetitorB displacement deals (time-limited, VP Sales approval required)
4. Update the ROI calculator to produce a $15,000+ justification narrative for champions before pricing is disclosed
