## Tax Guidance for Sellers

*The following is general information only. Participants should consult their personal tax advisors.*

| Scenario | Tax Treatment |
|----------|-------------|
| Shares held > 1 year from exercise | Long-term capital gains rate on gain above exercise price |
| Shares held < 1 year from exercise | Short-term capital gains rate (ordinary income) on gain |
| ISO shares — qualifying disposition | Capital gains treatment if holding period met (≥ 2 years from grant, ≥ 1 year from exercise) |
| ISO shares — disqualifying disposition | Spread at exercise subject to ordinary income + capital gains on appreciation |
| NSO shares | Ordinary income on spread at exercise (already recognized); capital gains on subsequent appreciation |

*Caution for ISO holders*: Exercising and immediately selling in this programme may be a disqualifying disposition. Consult a tax advisor before participating.
