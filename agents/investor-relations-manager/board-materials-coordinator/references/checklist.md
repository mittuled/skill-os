# Checklist: board-materials-coordinator

Covers every step required to produce, review, and distribute board meeting materials on schedule.

## 3 Weeks Before the Meeting

- [ ] Confirm board meeting date, time, and format (in-person, virtual, hybrid)
- [ ] Confirm all director availability — flag any conflicts immediately to the CEO
- [ ] Draft the board agenda with the CEO and board chair
- [ ] Identify all content sections and assign owners with internal deadlines
- [ ] Distribute section templates and data requirements to each owner
- [ ] Set internal deadline for first drafts: 10 business days before the meeting

## 2 Weeks Before the Meeting

- [ ] Section owner deadlines confirmed — send reminders if any are at risk
- [ ] CFO financials: verify close is complete and numbers are final before this step
- [ ] Consent items identified and legal drafts requested for routine resolutions

## 10 Days Before the Meeting (First Draft Due)

- [ ] All section drafts received (escalate to CEO if any section is not submitted)
- [ ] Numerical consistency check: verify that every metric appears consistently across all sections (ARR in the CEO update must match ARR in the CFO section)
- [ ] Narrative coherence review: does the full deck tell a consistent story? Do any sections contradict each other?
- [ ] Appropriate depth check: are all sections at board-level depth (strategic) rather than operational detail?
- [ ] "So what" check: does every data slide include a recommendation or decision request?

## 7 Days Before the Meeting

- [ ] Consolidated draft sent to CEO for final narrative review
- [ ] Consent resolutions finalized by legal and added to pre-read package
- [ ] Financial statements (P&L, balance sheet, cash flow) formatted and attached as appendix
- [ ] Supporting data appendix compiled (cap table, metrics detail, headcount summary)

## 5 Days Before the Meeting (Materials Due to Board)

- [ ] Final materials approved by CEO
- [ ] Distribution package assembled: deck + financial appendix + consent resolutions + agenda
- [ ] Distribution sent to all directors and observers via secure channel (board portal preferred, encrypted email if not)
- [ ] Delivery confirmation received from all directors (read receipts or portal access logs)
- [ ] Cover note sent with the materials highlighting: key discussion topics, decisions required, and page references

## 48 Hours Before the Meeting

- [ ] CEO briefed on: likely questions per director, contentious discussion topics, consent items requiring explanation, any director pre-meeting calls completed
- [ ] Any director pre-meeting questions received and routed to appropriate section owner
- [ ] Meeting logistics confirmed: video link tested, room booked, dial-in information distributed
- [ ] Physical materials prepared if in-person (printed deck, water, agenda on table)

## During the Meeting

- [ ] Record all decisions made (with quorum confirmation)
- [ ] Record all action items (owner, deliverable, due date)
- [ ] Note any dissenting votes on resolutions
- [ ] Note any material information shared verbally that is not in the pre-read materials

## Within 48 Hours Post-Meeting

- [ ] Draft minutes circulated to CEO and legal for review
- [ ] Minutes distributed to all directors for approval
- [ ] Action item tracker updated and distributed to all owners
- [ ] Follow-up materials requested by directors during the meeting compiled and sent

## Within 2 Weeks Post-Meeting

- [ ] Minutes approved and signed by the board chair or secretary
- [ ] Approved minutes filed in the corporate records
- [ ] Action items tracked — flag any that are at risk of missing their deadline
- [ ] Board meeting materials archived in the data room (board minutes folder)
