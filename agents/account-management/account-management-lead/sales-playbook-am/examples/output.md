## Executive Summary

This playbook standardises the TechFlow account management process across QBR execution, expansion conversations, renewal strategy, and escalation procedures. All four AMs (including three new hires) should treat this as the operating manual for their accounts.

## Section 1: Quarterly Business Review Template

**Recommended duration**: 60 minutes | **Cadence**: Quarterly

| Time | Agenda Item | Owner |
|------|-------------|-------|
| 0–10 min | Account health scorecard review | AM |
| 10–25 min | Usage and adoption deep-dive | AM + Customer |
| 25–35 min | Value delivered since last QBR | AM |
| 35–45 min | Roadmap alignment and upcoming releases | AM |
| 45–55 min | Expansion discussion (if applicable) | AM |
| 55–60 min | Next quarter commitments | Both |

**Health metrics to review each QBR**: NPS, weekly active users, feature adoption rate, deployment frequency.

## Section 2: Expansion Conversation Frameworks

### Upsell Framework
- **Opening**: "Based on your usage patterns, I wanted to share how TechFlow Dev Suite can help you achieve [specific outcome]."
- **Value anchor**: Tie to a business goal the customer stated in the last QBR.
- **Key objections**:

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "No budget this quarter" | Monthly billing available; annual commit unlocks volume discount. |
| "We haven't used what we have" | Review adoption together and identify quick wins first. |

### Cross-Sell Framework
- **Opening**: Same as upsell — lead with customer outcome, not product feature.
- **Key objections**:

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We use a different tool" | Integration is native — no workflow changes required. |
| "We need to evaluate options" | 30-day pilot available; setup under 2 hours. |

### Multi-Year Renewal Framework
- **Incentive structure**: 2-year = 5% discount + locked pricing; 3-year = 10% + dedicated CSM.

## Section 3: Renewal Strategy

**Timeline**:

| Weeks Before Renewal | Action |
|----------------------|--------|
| 12 weeks | Begin renewal health assessment |
| 8 weeks | Present renewal proposal and multi-year options |
| 4 weeks | Final negotiation and procurement engagement |
| 1 week | Signature chase and processing |

**Risk classification**:
- Low risk: NPS ≥ 8, usage trending up, no open escalations
- Medium risk: NPS 6–7 or flat usage
- High risk: NPS < 6 or usage decline > 15%

## Section 4: Escalation Procedures

**Escalation triggers**:
- NPS score below 6
- Support tickets unresolved > 7 days
- Executive sponsor change detected
- Competitor evaluation started
- Usage drop > 20% month-over-month

**Escalation path**:

| Level | Owner | Response Time |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| L1 | Account Manager | 24 hours |
| L2 | Account Management Lead | 4 hours |
| L3 | CBO / Executive Sponsor | 2 hours |
