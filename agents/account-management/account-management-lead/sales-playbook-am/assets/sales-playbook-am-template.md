### Seat Upsell

**Trigger**: Usage exceeds 80% of licensed seats, or customer reports users waiting for access.

| EXPAND Step | Execution |
|-------------|-----------|
| Explore | "What business goals require more team members to participate?" |
| X-ref | Map seat gap to the customer's stated growth objective |
| Pain-surface | Calculate cost of blocked users (lost productivity, missed SLA) |
| Anchor value | Quantify: "Adding X seats enables Y outcome worth $Z" |
| Navigate objections | "Budget constrained" → Show ROI per seat calculation |
| Drive to decision | Agree on seat count; send order form |
