# Checklist: Partner Activation Execution

## Purpose

Ensures each phase of partner activation is executed completely with no critical steps skipped. Use at each workflow step to verify completeness before advancing.

## Step 1: Partner Onboarding

### Portal and Access
- [ ] Partner portal account created with admin credentials
- [ ] Partner designated a partnership owner (name and contact confirmed)
- [ ] Product documentation shared (user guides, API docs, architecture overview)
- [ ] Demo environment provisioned with partner-specific configuration
- [ ] Slack channel or communication channel established

### Materials Delivery
- [ ] Sales enablement deck provided (ICP, value prop, competitive positioning)
- [ ] Joint battle cards delivered (positioning when selling alongside partner)
- [ ] Pricing guide shared (partner pricing, referral fees, or revenue share terms)
- [ ] Co-branding guidelines provided (logo usage, messaging standards)
- [ ] Case studies or reference customers shared

### Acknowledgment
- [ ] Partner confirms receipt of all materials
- [ ] Partner confirms designated owner and escalation contact
- [ ] Onboarding completion date recorded

## Step 2: Enablement Delivery

### Training Sessions
- [ ] ICP and buyer persona training completed
- [ ] Value proposition and messaging training completed
- [ ] Competitive positioning training completed
- [ ] Product demo training completed (partner can deliver a standalone demo)
- [ ] Objection handling training completed

### Certification
- [ ] Partner reps who completed training identified by name
- [ ] Certification criteria applied (demo quality, messaging accuracy, objection handling)
- [ ] Certified rep roster documented and shared with partner leadership
- [ ] Uncertified reps flagged with remediation plan
- [ ] Certification expiry and recertification cadence defined (recommended: annual)

## Step 3: Joint Pipeline Development

### Activity Execution
- [ ] Co-hosted webinar(s) scheduled with registration targets
- [ ] Joint prospecting list created (shared accounts identified, roles assigned)
- [ ] Warm introduction protocol defined (who introduces whom, format, SLA)
- [ ] Marketplace listing live (if applicable) with tracking configured
- [ ] Co-branded content published (blog posts, case studies, solution briefs)

### Pipeline Tracking
- [ ] "Partner-sourced" attribution tag configured in CRM
- [ ] "Partner-influenced" attribution tag configured in CRM
- [ ] Joint pipeline tracker created (shared spreadsheet or CRM view)
- [ ] Pipeline targets set for sourced vs. influenced (from activation plan)
- [ ] First pipeline review meeting scheduled

## Step 4: Performance Monitoring

### KPI Dashboard
- [ ] Leads generated tracked (weekly)
- [ ] Meetings booked tracked (weekly)
- [ ] Pipeline created tracked (weekly, sourced vs. influenced)
- [ ] Deals progressed tracked (stage advancement)
- [ ] 90-day milestone targets loaded for comparison

### Escalation Triggers
- [ ] Underperformance flag defined: <50% of target at week 4
- [ ] Remediation proposal template ready
- [ ] Escalation path defined (BD Manager → VP Sales → CBO)
- [ ] Partner-side escalation contact identified

## Step 5: Relationship Management

### Governance Cadence
- [ ] Weekly pipeline sync scheduled (BD + partner partnership owner)
- [ ] Monthly business review scheduled (BD + partner sales leadership)
- [ ] Quarterly executive check-in scheduled (VP Sales + partner VP)
- [ ] Meeting notes template established with action item tracking
- [ ] Action items from each meeting tracked to completion

### Blocker Management
- [ ] Technical integration blockers escalated to Engineering with SLA
- [ ] Rep disengagement addressed with partner leadership
- [ ] Lead quality issues documented with examples and feedback provided
- [ ] Blocker resolution tracked and reported in monthly review

## Health Check (Monthly)

| Dimension | Healthy | At Risk | Critical |
|-----------|---------|---------|----------|
| Pipeline generation | On target or above | 50-80% of target | <50% of target |
| Rep engagement | All certified reps active | Some reps inactive | Majority inactive |
| Governance | All meetings held | 1 meeting missed | 2+ meetings missed |
| Technical integration | Stable, no issues | Minor issues, workarounds | Blocking pipeline |
| Executive alignment | Quarterly check-in held | Check-in delayed | No executive contact |
