## 3. Content Production Workflow

```
Ideation → Brief → Draft → Edit → Design → Legal/Brand Review → Publish → Distribute → Measure
  2 days    1 day   3 days  2 days  1 day       1 day            D-day      D+1          Ongoing
```

### Roles & Responsibilities
| Step | Owner | SLA |
