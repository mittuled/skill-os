# Framework: content-engine-builder-marketing

Defines the content-to-funnel architecture, production workflow stages, distribution mapping, and repurposing model for a repeatable content production engine.

## Content-to-Funnel Mapping

| Funnel Stage | Goal | Content Types | ICP Trigger | Channel |
|-------------|------|---------------|-------------|---------|
| TOFU — Awareness | Reach and educate new audiences | Blog posts, industry reports, social content, podcast episodes, infographics | "I have a problem but haven't defined it" | SEO, social, paid promotion, syndication |
| MOFU — Consideration | Build preference and trust | Comparison guides, webinars, analyst reports, ROI calculators, demo videos, case study summaries | "I'm evaluating solutions" | Email nurture, retargeting, content hub |
| BOFU — Decision | Drive action and remove blockers | Full case studies, ROI calculators, free trials, competitive battle cards, implementation guides | "I'm ready to buy or need final proof" | Sales enablement, email (high-score leads), targeted ads |

**Rule**: Every content piece belongs to exactly one stage. Mix-stage content is an anti-pattern.

## Production Workflow

| Stage | Activity | Owner | SLA | Output |
|-------|----------|-------|-----|--------|
| 1. Ideation | Generate brief from editorial calendar topic; confirm keyword, audience, funnel stage, CTA | Demand Gen Manager | 1 day | Approved content brief |
| 2. Draft | Writer produces first draft per brief | Writer (internal or freelance) | 3–5 days | First draft |
| 3. Editorial review | Accuracy, tone, brand consistency, SEO check | Senior Content / Editor | 1–2 days | Marked-up draft |
| 4. SME review | Fact-check and domain accuracy on technical content | Internal SME | 2 days | Approved draft |
| 5. Design | Visual assets, formatting, CMS layout | Designer | 1–2 days | Designed content piece |
| 6. Approval | Final sign-off from content lead or VP Marketing (if strategic) | Content Lead | 1 day | Approved for publish |
| 7. Publish | CMS upload, metadata, internal links, UTMs, CTAs | Content Marketer | 1 day | Live URL |
| 8. Distribute | Push to all planned channels | Content Marketer | Same day as publish | Distribution confirmation |

**Total SLA**: TOFU blog post: 8–12 days. MOFU guide: 12–18 days. BOFU case study: 14–21 days.

## 90-Day Editorial Calendar Structure

| Week | Content Mix | Campaign Theme Alignment |
|------|------------|--------------------------|
| Weeks 1–4 | 2 TOFU, 1 MOFU | [Campaign theme 1] |
| Weeks 5–8 | 2 TOFU, 1 MOFU, 1 BOFU | [Campaign theme 2] |
| Weeks 9–12 | 2 TOFU, 1 MOFU, 1 BOFU | [Campaign theme 1 follow-up] |

**Minimum cadence**: 1 TOFU piece per week, 1 MOFU per 2 weeks, 1 BOFU per 4 weeks.

## Contributor Tiers and Brief Standards

| Contributor Type | Use Case | Brief Elements Required | Turnaround |
|----------------|----------|------------------------|------------|
| Internal writer | High-expertise, brand-critical content | Topic, audience, funnel stage, key messages (3), word count, CTA, references | 3–5 days |
| Freelance writer | Volume TOFU content | Same as internal + style guide link + 2–3 example pieces for tone | 5–7 days |
| Executive SME | Thought leadership, technical content | Key argument (1 paragraph), supporting points (bullet list), reviewer contact | Ghostwritten, 5 days |
| Agency | Campaign-scale production surges | Full brief + brand guidelines + content matrix | 7–14 days |

## Distribution Playbook by Content Type

| Content Type | Primary Channel | Repurpose Into | Paid Amplification | Sales Use |
|-------------|----------------|----------------|-------------------|-----------|
| Blog post | Company blog, email newsletter | Social snippets (5), LinkedIn article summary | Optional: promote top performers | Link in sequences |
| Long-form guide | Gated landing page | Blog teaser, social carousel, email 3-part series | Always amplify gated content | Share with active opportunities |
| Case study | Case study page, sales deck | Social proof pull quotes, 1-page PDF, email testimonial | Promote to lookalike audience | Primary sales enablement asset |
| Webinar / Video | YouTube, content hub | Blog recap post, social clips, email sequence | Promote as registration driver | Clip for demo calls |
| Infographic | Blog embed, social | Slide content, email visual | Boost high-engagement posts | Visuals in proposals |

## Engine Health Metrics

| Metric | Target | Warning Threshold | Action |
|--------|--------|-------------------|--------|
| On-time publish rate | ≥ 90% | < 80% | Diagnose production bottleneck |
| Content-to-MQL attribution | ≥ 20% of MQLs touch content | < 15% | Audit distribution coverage |
| TOFU organic traffic growth | ≥ 10% MoM | < 5% for 2+ months | SEO audit; keyword refresh |
| MOFU download rate | ≥ 3% of visitors | < 2% | CTA and offer optimization |
| BOFU-to-demo conversion | ≥ 10% of BOFU views | < 5% | Content quality or targeting issue |
