# Framework: conference-presence-manager

This framework defines the methodology for evaluating conference investment, designing booth strategy, managing sponsorship tiers, capturing leads, and measuring pipeline ROI.

## Conference Evaluation Matrix

Score each conference 1–5 on each criterion. Require composite ≥ 3.5/5 to recommend participation.

| Criterion | Weight | 1 (Fail) | 3 (Pass) | 5 (Strong) |
|-----------|--------|----------|----------|------------|
| ICP audience density | 30% | < 15% of attendees match ICP | 30–50% match ICP | > 60% match ICP |
| Historical pipeline attribution | 25% | No prior attendance data | Positive but < 3× cost | ≥ 5× investment in pipeline |
| Competitor presence | 15% | Competitor-dominated; no differentiation opportunity | 2–3 competitors present | Limited competitor presence; whitespace opportunity |
| Timing fit | 15% | Conflicts with major launches or quarter-close | Minor overlap managed | Ideal timing (Q1/Q3 pre-pipeline build) |
| Cost efficiency | 15% | Cost-per-lead > $500 (benchmark) | Cost-per-lead $200–500 | Cost-per-lead < $200 |

## Sponsorship Tier Evaluation

| Tier | Typical Benefits | Best For | ROI Expectation |
|------|-----------------|---------|----------------|
| Diamond / Presenting | Keynote speaking, premier booth placement, attendee list, branded session room | Marquee annual event for ICP category | 5–10× pipeline; brand authority |
| Platinum | Breakout session, premium booth, logo on key materials | High-ICP events where speaking access matters | 4–7× pipeline |
| Gold | Standard booth, logo placement, lead scanning | Mid-tier ICP events; brand awareness goal | 3–5× pipeline |
| Silver | Small booth or table-top, limited visibility | Testing new events; geo expansion | 2–3× pipeline |
| Attend-only | Staff registration, no booth | Intelligence gathering; competitive research | Not a pipeline goal |

**Negotiation add-ons to pursue regardless of tier:**
- Speaking slot in relevant breakout track
- Pre-event email blast to attendee list (opt-in)
- Lead scanning device included in booth package
- Sponsored social post by conference account
- Breakout room or hosted dinner inclusion

## Booth Strategy by Goal

| Primary Goal | Booth Design Focus | Staffing Priority | Lead Capture Method |
|-------------|-------------------|-----------------|--------------------|
| Pipeline generation | Demo station + meeting scheduling kiosk | AEs and SEs who can qualify and close | Badge scan + qualification form + meeting scheduler |
| Brand awareness | Visual dominance + thought leadership content | Brand-trained staff + executives | Badge scan + social engagement |
| Product launch | Live demo loop + launch announcement materials | SEs and PMM | Demo request form + launch interest capture |
| Competitive displacement | Differentiation messaging + comparison tools | AEs with competitive training | Qualification form targeting competitor customers |

## Demo Scheduling and Lead Capture Process

**Pre-event (3–4 weeks before):**
1. Identify target accounts attending using conference attendee list or LinkedIn event page
2. Send personalised pre-event outreach to top 20–50 target accounts: "Will you be at [Conference]? Let's meet."
3. Pre-book meetings for ≥ 50% of on-site meeting target before event opens

**On-site lead capture:**

| Lead Tier | Qualification Signal | Next Action | Follow-Up SLA |
|-----------|--------------------|-----------|--------------| 
| Hot (A) | Demo requested; pain confirmed; decision authority | AE follow-up within 24 hrs | 24-hour personalised email + meeting link |
| Warm (B) | Interest expressed; pain acknowledged; timeline unclear | SDR sequence within 48 hrs | 48-hour sequence with relevant content |
| Cold (C) | Badge scan only; no meaningful conversation | Nurture email sequence | 5-day nurture sequence |

## ROI Measurement Model

| Metric | Formula | Target Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-----------------|
| Cost-per-lead | Total event cost / total qualified leads | < $200 |
| Cost-per-meeting | Total event cost / meetings held on-site + booked from event | < $500 |
| Pipeline generated | Sum of opportunity values sourced from event leads | ≥ 3× total event investment |
| Pipeline conversion rate | Opportunities won / opportunities sourced | ≥ 25% within 6 months |
| Sponsor ROI ratio | Pipeline generated / sponsorship cost | ≥ 5× for tier commitment to renew |

## Booth Staffing and Role Assignments

| Role | Responsibility | Ratio |
|------|---------------|-------|
| Qualifier (SDR/BDR) | Engage walk-up traffic; qualify interest; route to AE | 1 per 100 expected booth visitors/day |
| Closer (AE) | Pre-booked meetings; escalated qualified conversations | 1 per 5 pre-booked meetings/day |
| Demo specialist (SE) | Live product demos; technical qualification | 1 per demo station |
| Booth anchor (Marketing) | Booth operations; swag management; lead capture | 1 at all times |
| Executive (optional) | High-value account meetings; keynote attendance | As available |

**Shift management**: No staff member should be on the booth floor for more than 3 consecutive hours. Schedule breaks and meal coverage explicitly.
