| Segment | Day 1 After | Day 3 After | Day 7 After |
|---------|------------|------------|------------|
| Hot leads (A) | Personalised AE email referencing conversation + meeting link | Follow-up call if no response | Deal review or SDR hand-off |
| Warm leads (B) | SDR email: relevant resource + meeting ask | Touch 2: case study or demo video | Touch 3: breakup email |
| Cold leads (C) | Nurture email: conference recap resource | Nurture touch 2 | Added to ongoing nurture programme |
| No-shows (registered, didn't attend) | "Sorry we missed you" + on-demand resource | Nurture touch 2 | — |
