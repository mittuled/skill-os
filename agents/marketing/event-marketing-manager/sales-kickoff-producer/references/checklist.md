# Checklist: sales-kickoff-producer

## Pre-Production (10–14 Weeks Out)

- [ ] SKO brief approved by CRO: objectives, format, dates, headcount, budget
- [ ] Venue or virtual platform confirmed and contracted
- [ ] Accommodation block secured (in-person) or platform licenses acquired (virtual)
- [ ] SKO theme approved by CEO and CRO
- [ ] Theme visual treatment approved by creative team
- [ ] Pre-SKO field survey launched to all reps: top objections, competitive challenges, skill gaps
- [ ] Content owner matrix confirmed (framework.md): all owners briefed and agreed

## Content Development (4–8 Weeks Out)

- [ ] Field survey results analysed and distributed to content owners
- [ ] Agenda framework approved by CRO (time allocation per module — framework.md targets)
- [ ] All content owners briefed on format, time limit, and expected output
- [ ] Quota letters and territory breakdowns delivered by Sales Ops
- [ ] Role-play scenarios drafted by Enablement with top AE input
- [ ] Battle cards and competitive positioning deck delivered by PMM
- [ ] Recognition categories and nomination process announced to the field
- [ ] Updated playbook in production; first draft reviewed by top AEs

## Materials and Pre-Reads (2–4 Weeks Out)

- [ ] All session decks delivered (first draft) and reviewed
- [ ] Pre-read packet compiled: playbook, battle cards, agenda, logistics
- [ ] Pre-reads distributed to all attendees at least 7 days before event
- [ ] Role-play scenario scripts and scoring rubrics distributed to facilitators
- [ ] Award nominees finalised; scripts drafted for presenter
- [ ] Run-of-show document drafted with minute-by-minute schedule and speaker transitions

## Logistics (2–3 Weeks Out)

- [ ] Travel and accommodation booked for all attendees (in-person)
- [ ] AV requirements confirmed: recording setup, breakout room mics, hybrid capability
- [ ] Catering confirmed with dietary accommodations
- [ ] Breakout room assignments mapped to session types
- [ ] On-site signage and branded materials ordered and delivery confirmed
- [ ] Session recording tools tested (video capture + transcript)

## Speaker Preparation (1–2 Weeks Out)

- [ ] All keynote speakers have completed at least 1 rehearsal
- [ ] All breakout session facilitators briefed on format and timing
- [ ] AV deck walk-through completed for all sessions in relevant rooms
- [ ] Presenter notes and timing guides distributed
- [ ] CRO approval of final agenda and recognition script

## Day-Of Execution

- [ ] Run-of-show printed and distributed to all event staff
- [ ] Recording devices active in all rooms before session start
- [ ] Session scribes in place for all strategic sessions
- [ ] Recognition materials (trophies, certificates, videos) staged and tested
- [ ] Real-time schedule enforcement: hard stop each session at planned time

## Post-SKO (Within 2 Weeks)

- [ ] Feedback survey deployed within 48 hours (session ratings, content usefulness)
- [ ] Session recordings processed and uploaded within 48 hours
- [ ] Post-SKO package assembled: recordings, updated playbook, action items, Q&A themes
- [ ] Post-SKO package delivered to CRO within 2 weeks
- [ ] 30-day reinforcement plan confirmed with Enablement (practice, coaching, follow-up content)
- [ ] Budget actuals reconciled; post-SKO report delivered
