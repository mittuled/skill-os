## Content Guidelines

### Formats Accepted

| Format | Platform | Minimum Quality | Length / Dimensions |
|--------|---------|----------------|-------------------|
| Video testimonial | TikTok, Instagram Reels | 720p minimum | 15-60s, 9:16 |
| Photo with product | Instagram, X | 1080px+ | Square or portrait |
| Tutorial / how-to | YouTube Shorts, TikTok | 720p minimum | 30-90s |
| Written review | LinkedIn, X | 100+ words | — |
| Story sequence | Instagram, TikTok | 720p minimum | 3-7 frames |

### Required Elements

- [ ] Product clearly visible within first 3 seconds (video) or central to image (photo)
- [ ] Creator's authentic voice — not a scripted ad read
- [ ] Brand mention: @[BrandHandle] or #[CampaignHashtag]
- [ ] Disclosure if any compensation received: [#gifted / #sponsored / #partner]

### Quality Standards (Minimum Bar)

- Good lighting — no dark, blurry, or shaky footage
- Audio clear and comprehensible (video)
- No competitor products featured prominently
- No hate speech, explicit content, or brand-unsafe scenarios

### Content Restrictions

- Do NOT make medical, legal, or financial claims about the product
- Do NOT reference specific pricing unless confirmed correct with brand team
- Do NOT use copyrighted music without cleared rights
- Do NOT impersonate brand employees or use brand trademarks in channel name

## Content Rights and Permissions

All UGC must be rights-cleared before republishing on brand channels.

### Rights Request Process

1. Identify UGC that meets quality standards
2. Comment on post OR DM creator: use approved rights request message (see below)
3. Creator replies with explicit written consent
4. Log consent in rights tracker with date, post URL, and creator handle
5. Republish with credit: "📸 @[creator handle]"

### Approved Rights Request Message

> "Hi [Name]! We love this — [specific genuine compliment about the content]. We'd love to feature it on our [channel/platform]. May we have permission to reshare it with full credit to you? Reply 'yes' to grant permission. Thank you!"

**Do NOT republish without written consent.** Screenshots of public posts do not constitute permission.
