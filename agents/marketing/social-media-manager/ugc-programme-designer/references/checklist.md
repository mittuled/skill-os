# Checklist: ugc-programme-designer — Programme Launch Readiness

## Phase 1: Programme Design (Weeks 1–2)

- [ ] Programme objectives defined: target volume, content types, platforms, business use cases
- [ ] Target contributor segments identified (power users, NPS promoters, community members)
- [ ] Participation mechanic selected (hashtag, ambassador programme, template prompts, or content series)
- [ ] Incentive and recognition structure designed with budget approved
- [ ] Content standards documented: quality bar, brand safety criteria, prohibited content types
- [ ] Usage rights framework defined for each repurposing use case (see framework.md)
- [ ] Rights acquisition templates drafted (comment script, email approval, release form)
- [ ] Rights log spreadsheet or system configured to track all permissions

## Phase 2: Legal and Compliance (Week 2–3)

- [ ] Usage rights templates reviewed by legal (ad creative and website use cases minimum)
- [ ] Programme guidelines document drafted covering brand rules, content standards, and rights
- [ ] Compensation and gifting policy aligned with legal (FTC disclosure if applicable)
- [ ] Release form approved by legal for Tier 1 Showcase content

## Phase 3: Programme Infrastructure (Week 3–4)

- [ ] UGC library / content management system configured (folders by theme, platform, tier)
- [ ] Monitoring queries configured to detect UGC submissions (hashtag + brand mention + product tags)
- [ ] Social listening alerts set for new programme hashtag
- [ ] Internal workflow established: submission → review → approval → amplification → rights log
- [ ] CS team briefed on programme: how to surface customer stories, outreach scripts
- [ ] Community manager (if applicable) briefed on programme and contributor invite process

## Phase 4: Seed and Launch (Week 4–5)

- [ ] Seed content created: 3–5 high-quality first-party posts demonstrating expected format
- [ ] Seed content published across target platforms before public programme launch
- [ ] Initial contributor outreach sent to 10–20 top power users and NPS promoters
- [ ] Programme launch post published with hashtag, prompt, and participation CTA
- [ ] Internal teams (sales, CS, product) notified with toolkit for amplifying the launch

## Phase 5: Ongoing Operations (Monthly)

- [ ] Submissions reviewed weekly for quality, brand safety, and tier classification
- [ ] Rights acquired for all Tier 1 and Tier 2 content before amplification
- [ ] Contributor acknowledgements sent within 24 hours of amplification
- [ ] UGC library updated with new curated content
- [ ] Monthly programme health report published (see framework.md for metrics)
- [ ] Underperforming mechanics identified and adjusted (submission rate < target triggers review)
- [ ] CS team check-in: new customer wins surfaced for outreach
