# Community-Led Growth Programme: Flowline Developer Community

## Scenario

Flowline's developer community has been live for 3 months with 1,840 members. The community manager wants to design a growth programme to increase membership organically. Given that most members are motivated by recognition and networking, and paid acquisition costs are rising, the team wants to launch an ambassador programme. The incentive budget is $3,000 for the pilot.

{

  "product": "Flowline",

  "member_motivations": ["recognition", "networking", "access"],
  "incentive_budget_usd": 3000,

}
