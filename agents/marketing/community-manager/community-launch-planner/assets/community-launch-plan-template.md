## Contingency Plans

| Scenario | Trigger | Response |
|----------|---------|----------|
| Low Day 1 signups (< 50% of target) | < 50% of target by noon | Activate secondary email blast to waitlist |
| Spam or abuse incident | Any report of harmful content | Moderator escalation path; temp pause public signup |
| Platform outage | > 15 min downtime | Post status update, push launch announcement by 24h |
| Negative press / social backlash | Any negative coverage | Community Manager drafts response; escalate to comms |
