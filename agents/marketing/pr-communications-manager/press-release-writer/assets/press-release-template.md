## Pre-Distribution Checklist

Use [`references/framework.md`](../references/framework.md) for detailed guidance on each item.

- [ ] Headline ≤10 words; leads with news; no superlatives
- [ ] Lead paragraph answers Who, What, When, Where, Why in ≤3 sentences
- [ ] Release is 400–600 words total
- [ ] AP Style applied throughout (numbers, dates, job titles, dollar amounts)
- [ ] At least one concrete metric in the lead or second paragraph
- [ ] Executive quote is attributable and not generic corporate boilerplate
- [ ] Boilerplate is current and matches company's standard boilerplate
- [ ] Contact information is accurate and includes a mobile number
- [ ] Legal review completed (especially for funding, M&A, or regulated industry releases)
- [ ] Executive approval obtained
- [ ] Wire service selected and account ready
- [ ] Embargo lift time confirmed with all embargoed contacts
- [ ] Wire distribution list reviewed (keywords, categories, geographies)
- [ ] Distribution timing set per framework guidelines (Tue–Thu, 9–11 a.m. for standard)
- [ ] SEO elements applied: keyword in headline, 2 links max with keyword-rich anchors
- [ ] "###" end marker present
