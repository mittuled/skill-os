## Relationship Status Definitions

| Status | Definition | Cadence Target |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| **Active** | Regular two-way contact; reporter covers the company | Monthly check-in; immediate response to queries |
| **Warm** | Past coverage or briefing; positive dialogue | Quarterly touch; pitch on relevant stories |
| **Cold** | No prior contact or lapsed relationship (>6 months) | Introduce via targeted pitch before requesting coverage |
| **Hostile** | Critical or consistently negative coverage | Monitor only; no unsolicited contact |

**Contact type definitions:**
- **Pitch** — Proactive story idea sent to reporter
- **Briefing** — Scheduled call to share news or company update
- **Background** — Off-record context provided; not for attribution
- **Event** — In-person / virtual event interaction
- **Query Response** — Response to inbound media request
- **Exclusivity** — Story offered to one outlet before broader distribution

**Scoring guide (1-5):**
- **5** — Champion; proactively covers company; strong personal rapport
- **4** — Positive relationship; covers company regularly when pitched
- **3** — Neutral; responds to pitches; no strong rapport yet
- **2** — Minimal contact; rarely responds; relationship needs investment
- **1** — No relationship established; treat as cold contact

## Privacy and Data Handling

- Store personal contact information per applicable data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA)
- Do not share contact database externally without explicit approval
- Review and purge contacts who have left their roles within 30 days of learning of the change
- Archive, do not delete, historical engagement log entries
