## Appendix: Report Significance Scoring

Use to prioritise which reports receive active participation and AR investment.

| Report | Analyst Firm | ICP Relevance | Buyer Influence Score | Competitive Presence | Overall Priority Score | Participation Decision |
|--------|-------------|--------------|----------------------|---------------------|----------------------|----------------------|
| | | [1-5] | [1-5] | [1-5] | [Sum / 15] | [Full / Limited / Monitor only] |

**Scoring guide**:
- **ICP Relevance** (1-5): Does our target buyer read this report in their purchase process?
- **Buyer Influence** (1-5): How often is this report cited in deals or requested by prospects?
- **Competitive Presence** (1-5): Are key competitors featured? Does absence hurt us in deals?

**Thresholds**: Score ≥12: full participation required. Score 7-11: participate if resources allow. Score <7: monitor only.
