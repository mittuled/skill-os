## Compliance Reminders

1. Never incentivise reviews with discounts or gifts — violates platform terms of service
2. Do not pre-screen customers before asking for reviews (only sending to known promoters violates G2 guidelines)
3. Always allow customers to leave any star rating — do not guide to 5-star only
