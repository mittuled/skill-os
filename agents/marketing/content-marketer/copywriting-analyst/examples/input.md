# Scenario: Landing Page Copy Review for Project Management SaaS

Review the following landing page copy for TaskFlow, a B2B SaaS project management tool targeting engineering teams at mid-market companies (200-2000 employees).

## Target Voice Profile

- Formality: 60% formal / 40% casual
- Tone: 70% serious / 30% playful
- Authority: 50% respectful / 50% irreverent
- Energy: 60% enthusiastic / 40% matter-of-fact

## Copy to Analyse

**Headline**: "Project Management That Actually Works"

**Subheadline**: "Stop wasting time on tools that slow you down. TaskFlow keeps your engineering team shipping."

**Body**:
TaskFlow is a project management platform that was built specifically for engineering teams. Our solution is designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing development workflow, including GitHub, GitLab, and Jira.

Features include automated sprint planning, real-time velocity tracking, and customisable kanban boards. The platform is used by teams at companies of all sizes.

TaskFlow can be deployed in minutes and requires no complex configuration. Our customer success team is available to help with onboarding.

**CTA**: "Learn More"
