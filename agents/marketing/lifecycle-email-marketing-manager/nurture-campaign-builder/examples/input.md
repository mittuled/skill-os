# Scenario: MQL Nurture Sequence for Enterprise Data Platform

Build a nurture campaign for a B2B data platform company targeting mid-market data engineering leaders who have downloaded a whitepaper on "Modern Data Stack Architecture" but have not requested a demo.

## Context

- **Product**: Cloud-native data pipeline platform, $50K-200K ACV
- **Target segment**: VP/Director of Data Engineering at companies with 200-2,000 employees
- **Entry trigger**: Downloaded "Modern Data Stack Architecture 2024" whitepaper
- **Monthly MQL volume**: ~150 leads
- **Current MQL-to-SQL rate**: 5.1%
- **Target MQL-to-SQL rate**: 10%
- **Sales cycle**: 60-90 days from first meeting to close

## Available Content Assets

- Blog: "5 Signs Your ETL Pipeline Is Holding You Back"
- Webinar recording: "From Batch to Streaming: A Migration Playbook"
- Comparison guide: "DataPlatform vs. Fivetran vs. Airbyte"
- Case study: "How Acme Corp Reduced Data Latency by 80%"
- ROI calculator: Interactive tool showing cost savings vs. legacy ETL
- Free sandbox: 14-day trial environment with sample datasets
