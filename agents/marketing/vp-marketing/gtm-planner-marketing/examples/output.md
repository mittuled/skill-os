## Executive Summary

SentinelGuard Enterprise targets IT Security Directors and CISOs at 500–5,000 employee regulated enterprises. The $350K marketing budget is allocated across 6 channels with partner referrals and PR/earned media ranked highest by ROI index. Launch date is 2026-05-01. Pipeline target is $4.8M marketing-sourced, requiring approximately 240 MQLs across the launch and post-launch phases.

## Phased Timeline

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Weeks -6 to -1, March 19 – April 30)

**Budget:** $70,000 (20%)
**Objective:** Build awareness, prime the analyst and media community, arm sales

| Activity | Channel | Owner | Due Date |
|---|---|---|---|
| Analyst briefings (Gartner, Forrester) | PR/Earned | PR Manager | April 10 |
| Partner activation briefs | Partner Referral | Partner Manager | April 15 |
| Launch blog post + SEO landing page | Content/SEO | Content Manager | April 20 |
| Sales deck and competitive battlecard | Sales Enablement | Product Marketing | April 22 |
| Webinar registration page live | Webinars | Marketing Ops | April 25 |
| Paid search campaigns staged in platform | Paid Search | Paid Media | April 28 |

**Gate:** All assets approved; sales briefed; press embargo confirmed.

### Phase 2: Launch (Week 0, May 1)

**Budget:** $175,000 (50%)
**Objective:** Maximum market impact with synchronized channel activation

| Activity | Channel | Activation Time |
|---|---|---|
| Press release distribution | PR/Earned | 08:00 |
| Partner co-announcement emails | Partner Referral | 08:30 |
| Email to full prospect database | Email Nurture | 09:00 |
| Paid search campaigns activate | Paid Search | 09:00 |
| Launch webinar (live) | Webinars | 12:00 |
| Social announcement posts | Paid Social | 09:00 |

**Gate:** All channels live; war room monitoring active T+0 to T+48h.

### Phase 3: Post-Launch (Weeks +1 to +8, May 8 – June 26)

**Budget:** $105,000 (30%)
**Objective:** Sustain momentum, optimize winners, build pipeline velocity

Key activities: Double down on highest-converting channels (week 2 data), run follow-up webinar, activate event sponsorships, publish 3 customer case studies.

**Gate:** Performance review at T+30 days; budget reallocations approved.
