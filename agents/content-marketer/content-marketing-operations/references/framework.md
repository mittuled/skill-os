# Framework: content-marketing-operations

Defines the production status model, CMS publishing checklist, SEO integration standards, and distribution execution playbook for ongoing content operations.

## Weekly Production Status Model

| Status | Definition | Required Action |
|--------|-----------|----------------|
| On Track | Draft due date > 3 days away; no blockers | Monitor |
| At Risk | Draft due within 3 days; SME review not started | Alert content lead; escalate if not resolved in 24 hours |
| Blocked | Waiting on approval, design, or SME for > 2 days | Identify substitute path or defer publish date |
| Delayed | Missed publish date | Document root cause; reschedule within 5 business days |
| Complete | Published, distributed, tracked | Log to content library |

## CMS Publishing Checklist

Every piece must clear all checks before publishing.

| Check | Standard | Fail Action |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| Title tag | 50–60 characters; includes primary keyword | Rewrite |
| Meta description | 140–160 characters; includes CTA or hook | Rewrite |
| H1 | Matches or closely mirrors title; primary keyword included | Rewrite |
| Body keyword density | Primary keyword 1–2% of body; LSI keywords present | Review |
| Internal links | Minimum 2 internal links to related content | Add links before publish |
| CTA | At least 1 in-body CTA aligned to funnel stage | Add CTA |
| Tracking UTM | UTM on all outbound links in content and distribution | Add UTMs |
| Mobile rendering | Preview in mobile view before publish | Fix layout |
| Image alt text | All images have descriptive alt text | Add alt text |
| Author and date | Author name and publish date visible | Add metadata |
| Schema markup | Article schema applied (blog posts, guides) | Add if platform supports |

## SEO Integration Standards

| Content Type | Target Keyword Volume | Minimum DA for Outranking | Content Length | Freshness |
|-------------|----------------------|--------------------------|----------------|-----------|
| TOFU blog post | 500–5,000 mo. searches | Competitive analysis required | 1,000–2,000 words | Review every 12 months |
| MOFU pillar page | 500–2,000 mo. searches | Less critical; lead gen focus | 2,000–4,000 words | Review every 6 months |
| BOFU case study | Branded/long-tail only | Not applicable | 800–1,500 words | Update quarterly |
| Comparison page | 100–1,000 mo. searches (high intent) | Medium competition acceptable | 1,500–3,000 words | Review every 3 months |

## Distribution Execution Checklist

Execute in this sequence after publishing:

| Step | Action | Channel | Timing |
|------|--------|---------|--------|
| 1 | Share internal Slack announcement | Internal | Same day |
| 2 | Post to LinkedIn company page | LinkedIn | Same day |
| 3 | Queue Twitter/X thread (if applicable) | Twitter/X | Same day |
| 4 | Add to next email newsletter digest | Email | Within 1 week |
| 5 | Add to content library in sales enablement tool | Sales | Same day |
| 6 | Notify relevant sales reps (for BOFU content) | Sales DM/Slack | Same day |
| 7 | Submit to content syndication partners (if applicable) | 3rd-party | Within 2 days |
| 8 | Set up paid promotion (for MOFU/BOFU top performers) | Paid social | Within 1 week |
| 9 | Repurpose into derivative formats (see below) | Multiple | Within 2 weeks |

## Content Repurposing Matrix

| Source Content | Derivative 1 | Derivative 2 | Derivative 3 |
|---------------|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| Long-form guide (2,000+ words) | 5-part email series | LinkedIn carousel | 3-part social thread |
| Webinar (recorded) | Blog recap post | Short-form video clips (3–5 clips) | Social pull quotes |
| Case study | 1-page PDF | Social proof pull quote | Email testimonial insert |
| Research report | Infographic | Press release data points | Blog deep-dives (one per finding) |
| Blog post series | Gated ebook compilation | Email nurture sequence | Social series |

## Performance Review Triggers

| Signal | Threshold | Action |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Organic traffic drop | > 20% MoM | SEO audit; check for ranking changes |
| Low organic CTR | < 2% at ranking position ≤ 10 | Rewrite title tag and meta |
| High bounce rate | > 75% on content pages | Review content quality and matching intent |
| No MQL attribution | 0 MQLs from a BOFU piece after 30 days | Review CTA placement, targeting, promotion |
| High engagement, low conversion | Top-10 traffic page, 0 MQLs | Audit CTA and offer alignment to audience intent |
