## 2. Workflow & Process Map

### Standard Article Workflow (SLA: 12 business days)

| Stage | Owner | SLA | Definition of Done |
|-------|-------|-----|--------------------|
| Topic research + brief | Content lead | Day 1–2 | Brief approved in [PM tool] |
| Draft | Writer | Day 3–7 | Google Doc shared for review |
| Editor review | Editor | Day 8–9 | Comments resolved; clean copy |
| SEO optimization | SEO specialist | Day 9–10 | Meta title, description, links, schema added |
| Visual design | Designer | Day 10–11 | Hero image + in-article graphics approved |
| Final approval | Content Manager | Day 12 | Approved in CMS; publish scheduled |
| Distribution | Social / Email | Day 12–13 | Scheduled across all channels |

### Fast-Track Workflow (SLA: 5 business days — breaking news / reactive content)
1. Brief approved same day
2. Draft due within 2 days
3. Condensed edit + SEO pass in 1 day
4. Publish without design asset (stock image acceptable)
5. Design asset added within 48 hours post-publish

## 7. Quality Assurance Checklist

Before every publish, verify:

- [ ] Headline: 50–60 characters; contains primary keyword
- [ ] Meta description: 140–160 characters; CTA included
- [ ] H1 unique; H2s contain secondary keywords
- [ ] All links checked (no broken or outdated)
- [ ] Images: compressed, alt text complete
- [ ] Author bio + headshot added
- [ ] CTA block included (lead magnet or demo link)
- [ ] UTM parameters on all tracked links
- [ ] Social share preview (OG tags) verified
- [ ] Spelling and grammar: zero errors
- [ ] Brand voice: reviewed against style guide
