# Scenario: Post-Launch Accessibility Audit of SaaS Onboarding Flow

Conduct a full accessibility audit on a B2B SaaS onboarding flow that:

1. Spans 5 screens: sign-up form, email verification, workspace setup, team invite, and first-project wizard
2. Targets WCAG 2.1 AA conformance
3. Must support web (Chrome, Safari, Firefox) on desktop and mobile viewports
4. Uses a custom design system with branded colour tokens
5. Has received 3 support tickets in the past month mentioning "can't tab through the sign-up form"
