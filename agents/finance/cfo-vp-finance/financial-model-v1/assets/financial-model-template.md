## Revenue Model

[Bottoms-up revenue forecast with driver definitions and cohort-level detail.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: "Revenue builds from 50 inbound leads/month at 8% conversion, $18K ACV, with 115% NRR driving expansion from month 6 cohorts onward"

- Format: Driver table → monthly cohort matrix → summary chart]

## Cost Structure

[P&L model with COGS, gross margin, and OpEx by department.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: "COGS: $2.1K/customer/year (AWS $1.2K, support $600, API costs $300); Gross margin: 78% improving to 82% at scale"
- Bad: "Costs are 40% of revenue"
- Format: COGS waterfall → OpEx by department with headcount detail → margin summary]

## Cash Flow Statement

[Monthly cash flow with billing mix, collections timing, and runway calculation.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: "65% annual prepay / 35% monthly billing; DSO 38 days; cash conversion cycle modelled monthly through month 24"
- Bad: "Cash equals revenue minus expenses"
- Format: Monthly cash flow table → runway chart → cash conversion cycle metrics]

## SaaS Metrics Dashboard

[Key SaaS metrics with calculation methodology and benchmarks.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: "ARR $2.4M (month 24); NRR 118%; LTV/CAC 4.2x (gross margin basis); CAC payback 14 months; burn multiple 1.8x"
- Bad: "MRR is growing"
- Format: Metric definition table → current values → 6-month trend → benchmark comparison]

## Scenario Analysis

[Best/base/worst scenarios with driver variations and trigger definitions.

GUIDANCE:
- Good: "Worst case varies: growth rate -30%, churn +2pp, CAC +25%; triggers scenario if two consecutive months miss pipeline targets by >20%"
- Bad: "Optimistic scenario: everything goes well"
- Format: Scenario comparison table → trigger definitions → management action plans]
