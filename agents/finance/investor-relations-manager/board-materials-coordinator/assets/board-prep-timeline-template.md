## Quality Checklist

Before final distribution:

- [ ] All metrics consistent across slides (no conflicting numbers)
- [ ] Financial statements tied to FP&A approved version
- [ ] Each substantive slide has a "so what" or decision requested
- [ ] No confidential customer names or PII visible
- [ ] Executive summary leads with key takeaways
- [ ] Consent resolutions are attached and complete
