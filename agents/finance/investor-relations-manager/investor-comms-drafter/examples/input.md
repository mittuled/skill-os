# Investor Comms Drafter — Example Input

## Scenario

Meridian AI just hired a VP of Engineering (Sarah Chen, ex-Notion). This is a material hire that investors have been asking about — the company has been operating without a VP Eng for 6 months. The IR Manager needs to draft a brief announcement to all investors, framing the hire as a milestone and indicating when Sarah starts.

## Input JSON

```json
{
  "company_name": "Meridian AI",
  "comm_type": "executive_change",
  "subject": "Meridian AI — VP Engineering hire: Sarah Chen joins April 14",
  "audience": "all_investors",
  "date": "2026-03-31",
  "sender_name": "Alex Chen, CEO",
  "person_name": "Sarah Chen",
  "role": "VP Engineering",
  "change_type": "appointment",
  "highlights": [
    "Sarah Chen (ex-Notion, ex-Figma) joins as VP Engineering on April 14",
    "Sarah led Notion's infrastructure team from 5 to 45 engineers over 3 years",
    "Closes a 6-month gap in engineering leadership that has been a recurring investor question"
  ],
  "body_notes": "Frame this as an execution milestone, not just a hire. Note that Sarah will immediately own the infrastructure roadmap and the team will report to her from day one. Address the 6-month gap proactively.",
  "asks": [
    "If you know anyone in Sarah's network at Notion or Figma who we should loop in as we scale the team, please make the intro"
  ]
}
```
