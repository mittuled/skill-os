# Checklist: user-conference-producer

## Vision and Planning (9–12 Months Out)

- [ ] Conference brief approved by leadership: theme, target attendance, audience mix, business outcomes
- [ ] Venue shortlisted and site visits completed
- [ ] Venue contract signed; hotel room blocks secured
- [ ] Conference budget approved by finance
- [ ] Sponsor outreach strategy defined: target sponsors by tier with revenue targets

## Speaker and Content (5–8 Months Out)

- [ ] Call for papers published with submission deadline, criteria, and topic areas
- [ ] Target customer speaker list compiled (minimum 10 confirmed customer speakers for agenda credibility)
- [ ] Internal speakers identified for keynote, product, and workshop sessions
- [ ] Speaker invitations sent for curated sessions
- [ ] 60%+ of speaker roster confirmed
- [ ] Diamond and Platinum sponsors committed
- [ ] Sponsor content review process defined: all sponsor session abstracts require editorial approval

## Registration and Promotion (4–5 Months Out)

- [ ] Registration page live with early-bird pricing and speaker teasers
- [ ] Promotional campaign launched: email, social, sales outreach, partner cross-promotion
- [ ] Registration tracking dashboard active with weekly pace-vs-target monitoring
- [ ] Partner co-promotion agreements in place

## Agenda and Production (2–4 Months Out)

- [ ] Full agenda published on conference website
- [ ] All speakers confirmed with signed speaker agreements
- [ ] Gold and Silver sponsors committed and onboarded
- [ ] Session materials production underway (speaker decks, workshop guides)
- [ ] Mobile app or digital agenda configured
- [ ] Networking programming designed: roundtables, evening events, expo hours

## Pre-Event (4–6 Weeks Out)

- [ ] All speaker decks in final review
- [ ] Logistics finalised: registration desk staffing, room assignments, AV requirements per room
- [ ] Attendee communications active: weekly emails with agenda highlights, logistics
- [ ] On-site staff briefed with role assignments
- [ ] Expo floor map finalised with sponsor booth assignments
- [ ] Catering confirmed with attendee dietary requirements

## Final Preparation (1–2 Weeks Out)

- [ ] Run-of-show finalised and distributed to all event staff and speakers
- [ ] All session feedback collection mechanisms tested (mobile app / QR codes)
- [ ] Post-event survey drafted and ready to send within 24 hours of event close
- [ ] Post-event sales follow-up sequences built and ready to launch within 48 hours
- [ ] PR/media briefings complete if press attending

## On-Site Execution

- [ ] Setup complete day before; all AV tested in all session rooms
- [ ] Registration desk staffed and systems functional
- [ ] Real-time session attendance tracking active
- [ ] Real-time feedback collection live in each session room
- [ ] Sponsor booth setup confirmed before expo opens
- [ ] Networking events confirmed: roundtable seating charts, evening event logistics

## Post-Conference (Within 2 Weeks)

- [ ] Post-event survey deployed within 24 hours
- [ ] Thank-you emails to all attendees deployed within 48 hours (with session recordings)
- [ ] Sales follow-up sequences launched within 48 hours for prospect attendees
- [ ] NPS and session ratings compiled
- [ ] Sponsor feedback collected; renewal conversations initiated
- [ ] Pipeline from conference attendees tagged in CRM
- [ ] Post-conference report delivered within 2 weeks: attendance, NPS, pipeline, sponsor revenue, recommendations
